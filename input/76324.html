DOSE: 200 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 215 lb


I have done only a few other drugs and this is probably not in top 3 for me...I've done hydrocodone a pill I haven't heard of anywhere but here called L's xanax klonopin and marijuana several times, but this is worth trying.

12:00 pm
Took 4 50mg pills ate a pb&j and watched Fox News nothing yet.

12:30
Started feeling really light...Starting to feel pretty good. Mellow and just peaceful.

1:15
Starting to feel very relaxed, I'm starting to feel a nice body high just...great.

1:30
I think I might be at the plateau I just feel really...peaceful and lazy and mellowed out.

2:00
Very euphoric and cant help from smiling. I feel great. Just...ahh =]

2:48
I think Im coming down. Not bad at all overall the whole thing is feeling like a hydrocodone trip without getting the nods. I love it.

3:30
Welp its kinda wearing off. Still feel great. Body high is still there. Just feel peaceful.

4:10
Welp back to normal. Still feeling great. just a little nauseated. But it was pretty good. =]
