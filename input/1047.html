Horny as Hell
5-MeO-DiPT
by Moongoose
Citation:   Moongoose. "Horny as Hell: experience with 5-MeO-DiPT (ID 1047)". Erowid.org. Aug 5, 2000. erowid.org/exp/1047

 
DOSE: 11 mg oral 5-MeO-DiPT (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 84 kg


Ona nice friday evening I ingested a first dose of 8.5 mg 5 Meo-Dipt orally. The taste was nice. I was on a party in a outside environment with a nice fire and lots of cool people. After ingesting the first effects developed relatively fast. 45 min .. I REALLY LIKED IT.

As the evening wore on I noticed that I was in a constant state of dualism between riding the trip individually or letting my tripping spirit float away because I WAS HORNY AS HELL and wanted a women. Time after time this cycle happened. Sometimes I got an increase in initiative energy and found myself jumping from one adventure into the other. Riding the bull, sitting on the bar slamming tequilas, shooting the gun or climbing on the DJ platform. About 1 1/2 hour into the experience I snorted about 3 mg 5-MeO-DiPT which made a nice contribution to the overall effect. I really find this substance to make you horny but it also makes you trip really nice. I didn't sleeep for two days because I didn't want to and smoked some marijuana during the comedown.

Loved it , nice compound !
