I just wanted to say that I have taken many drug tests in the past while on DXM without a hitch. When I say 'on DXM', I mean that I had dosed about an hour and a half to two hours before the test.

I have one of the tests and it said that the following were tested for:
Cocaine
Marijuana
Opiates/Morphine
Amphetamines
Methamphetamine
Phencyclidine
Benzodiazepine
Barbituate
Oxycodone
Methylenedioxymethamphetamine

It was a urine test done in a lab.

Tolerance MAY be an issue. I had been taking DXM fairly regularly for approximately four years. DXM is great. It drains my soul, but it's fun at the time!
