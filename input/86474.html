Empty. Lost.
Escitalopram (Lexapro)
by Adubz
Citation:   Adubz. "Empty. Lost.: experience with Escitalopram (Lexapro) (ID 86474)". Erowid.org. Jul 21, 2010. erowid.org/exp/86474

 
DOSE: 20 mg oral Pharms - Escitalopram (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 255 lb


I feel as though I should share this experience as many others may be in my position, lost, in a haze, reality-lost, and overall emotionless. That's why they gave it to me. So I wouldn't kill myself, so I wouldn't feel sad. I stare in the mirror and see nothing. I think about sad things and my mind instantly sedates my emotions. That's why I haven't cried in months. That's why people think I'm uninterested in anything. Everyone thinks I'm strung out on drugs.
