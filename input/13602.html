I Felt Like Crap.... Now I Feel Good
Trazodone
by Trazodone
Citation:   Trazodone. "I Felt Like Crap.... Now I Feel Good: experience with Trazodone (ID 13602)". Erowid.org. Sep 14, 2005. erowid.org/exp/13602

 
DOSE: 200 mg oral Pharms - Trazodone (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


I wantd to see what trazodone was like ever since I saw it in my medicine cupboard. Well, it was bout 4 pm and I snuck into my kitchen and into the medicine cabinet and took out 4 pills of trazodone which were 50 mg each. I did not know what I was getting myself into. I took all of them with milk and about 30 minutes later I felt the effects.

I was not feeling so good! I felt like I was drunk, but it was not a good drunk. With the drunkeness feeling came nausea. I immediately told my parents I was not feeling fine and I ran up to my room. I decided to watch some tv and that was all I wanted to do. I could not get up or I felt like I would throw-up or collapse on the ground. I went downstairs and got myself a snack trying to act normal. If I would have walked another step I would have barfed! I ate a little of pretzels and then just wanted to sleep. I fell asleep, with a horrible day awaiting.

I woke up feeling like shit, the same way it was the night before but only worser. I told my mom I felt sick and that I wanted to stay home, and so I did. That whole day I felt like shit and wanted to do nothing. By the end of the day the effects were wearing off and I was feeling better.

The next day I felt very good for some reason. That whole day I felt great and nothing could piss me off.

My experience was not good in my opinion and I dont think that I will try it again due to the drunken and nauseating effects.
