I Bit Him in the Leg
PCP
by King Nothing
Citation:   King Nothing. "I Bit Him in the Leg: experience with PCP (ID 15402)". Erowid.org. Jul 24, 2002. erowid.org/exp/15402

 
DOSE: 5 hits smoked PCP

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


My parents were out for the night and I had nothing to do so I called my buddy and he came over with some PCP he stole from his dad's stash. We went to my room and we smoke it off my pipe. I got 5 hits, and I was sweating like a pig. I went to the bathroom to wash my face off and I looked in the mirror and didn't see my reflection. I felt my teeth and I had fangs, and then I began to believe that I was a vampire.

I walked back into my room and my friend was listening to some music on my Discman and I bit him in the leg. He yelled at me. I did not let go so he kicked me and I hit my head on the post of my bed (which severely hurt). That made me realize I wasn't a vampire. The rest of the trip was safer and fun indeed, but my friend has a scar on his leg now. My suggestion would be to have somebody there with you (not on PCP) to make sure you don't get out of control.
