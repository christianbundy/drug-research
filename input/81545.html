A Trip While Tripping
DOM & 2C-B
by amfournda
Citation:   amfournda. "A Trip While Tripping: experience with DOM & 2C-B (ID 81545)". Erowid.org. Oct 23, 2012. erowid.org/exp/81545

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 20 mg oral 2C-B (powder / crystals)
  T+ 0:00 15 mg oral DOM (powder / crystals)
  T+ 1:00 15 mg oral DOM (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


A little background first: I have tried 10mg DOM once before. The effects were very mild. This time around I was looking to have a very powerful experience. That is why the dosages are so high. They are not mistakes. I apparently have a very high tolerance. Times are also approximate. I was not attempting to keep exact track of time.

(T+0:00) Took 20mg 2C-B and 15mg DOM upon getting to the party.

(T+1:00) First effects, body buzz and feeling of energy. There was also strong nausea, I felt somewhat sick but did not vomit.

(T+1:00) Took 15mg DOM. It is easy to get fixated on objects, people and conversations. Nausea is fading. [Erowid Note: 30 mgs of DOM is considered an extremely high dose, see DOM Dose Chart]

(T+2:00) Nausea is gone, definite 'high' feeling but no hallucinations yet. Went out to get a hamburger in a sit-down restaurant. It seemed noisy and colors were pronounced.

(T+3:30) Got back to the party, there was some psy-trance music playing and trip-art all over the walls.

(T+5:00) It was very difficult for me to keep track of time, it seemed much later than it was.

(T+7:00) Mental confusion, I would start walking some place and wonder why I was going there. Couldnt remember reason.

(T+9:00) Sitting in chair listening to psy-trance and looking at trip art, it is very difficult for me to understand a conversation, although I apparently (according to my sitter) still gave reasonable responses to questions. Felt very disjointed.

(T+10:00) Leave party, start to come down, any visuals I had go away.

(T+11:00) Go to sleep, it is restless and I keep waking up.

I woke up in the morning simply feeling slightly tired and a little stoned feeling.

All in all, this was a good experience, however I probably wont repeat it considering the dosages I had to take. I would recommend this drug to people who like mushrooms and/or acid but want to try something different.
