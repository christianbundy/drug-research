A Little Warning
Oxycodone
by Ryan
Citation:   Ryan. "A Little Warning: experience with Oxycodone (ID 18043)". Erowid.org. Oct 9, 2002. erowid.org/exp/18043

 
DOSE: 60 mg oral Pharms - Oxycodone (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 165 lb


I was in car accident 2 weeks ago and I broke my femur and hip and had to have intense surgery. In the hospital I was on all kinds of pain medicine, morphine, toradol and something begining with a d. But when I was sent home the doctor sent me home with percocet 5/325 to take 2 every 4 hours as needed for pain. I was in extreme pain my first few days so I was taking 2 pills every 4 hours faithfully for about a week. The pain has gone down alot and I really dont need the pills anymore but I feel like I need them...like I'm addicted. So I came back to my favorite site Erowid to see what my percocet pills were all about. I find about that the 5 is 5 mg of oxycodone and 325 tylenol (acetaminophen) so I have been taking 60mg of oxycodone a day. I am now goin to take one every 4 hours and then half every 4 hours and try to get my self off these pills. I feel physically addicted to these pills and a little mentally. A little warning to people going to take percocet for pain...be careful!
