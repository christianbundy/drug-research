DOSE: 35 mg rectal Methiopropamine (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 60 kg


I've tried insufflating MPA for help on my studies, but it didn't produce any noticable good effects, all I experienced was chest tightness and a need to redose.

I've read that by plugging MPA, you get better effects, so I disolated about 35mg in 10ml of luke water and administered it to myself. I watched an episode while I waited lying in bed on my belly.

20 min later mild effects were noticable, talkativeness, mild euphoria, I felt energized and not sleepy anymore.
This lasted for about 2 hours, I didn't redose, even tho I wanted.

In conclusion I would say that sniffing methiopropamine is kind of a waste, if you have the ability to plug it, try doing that instead.

I wanted to forget about that bag of mpa, but apparently it will come in handy on sleepy days.
