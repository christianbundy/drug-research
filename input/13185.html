What Day Is It?
Paroxetine (Paxil)
by Cruisr3k
Citation:   Cruisr3k. "What Day Is It?: experience with Paroxetine (Paxil) (ID 13185)". Erowid.org. Jun 26, 2007. erowid.org/exp/13185

 
DOSE:   repeated oral Pharms - Paroxetine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


Last Friday I found these pills I hadn't had in a while and since I was bored I decided to try some. I was talking on the phone with a friend because it sucks to be high and alone, but I didn't want to take it out with my friends and it not do anything. I popped 3 or so 20mg in my mouth and after a while I started getting a cool high. A general euphoria with about 1/2second delay in sight and slight dizziness. Well I considered side effects good at the time since nothing bad had happened so of course I decided to pop some more. I got extremely tired after about an hour or two but decided to stay up late.

About two hours later I decided to go to sleep, but the sleepiness had worn off and I didn't fall asleep for a lot longer and had slight nausea. After that I remember waking up Sunday and wondering what had happened. I didn't fall in a coma because friends told me they saw me awake and actually talked to me. So now I have lost 24 hours of memory because of those pills.
