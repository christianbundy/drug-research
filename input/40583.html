Notes on Dosage
Kratom (Mitrogyna speciosa)
by Ryan
Citation:   Ryan. "Notes on Dosage: experience with Kratom (Mitrogyna speciosa) (ID 40583)". Erowid.org. Feb 14, 2005. erowid.org/exp/40583

 
DOSE:   oral Kratom (ground / crushed)


I received regular kratom leaves (not labeled 'super') from an online vendor. I am a former opiate addict, so I can make a comparison that 4-6 grams of leaf probably equals 100mg of codeine sulfate. If I eat more than 6 grams I get nausea.

I don't make tea out of the leaves, I crush and eat them whole. I even have tolerance to kratom, as I've been doing it twice a day for about a month, and 7 grams would still make me sick, causing me to feel like I've had too much opiates (but I can handle 40mg oxycodone fine).

I wanted to warn people that taking higher doses of potent kratom leaves could make someone very, very sick.
