Daughter of the Egyptian Lilly
Blue Lotus (Dauben Water Lilly)
by isis
Citation:   isis. "Daughter of the Egyptian Lilly: experience with Blue Lotus (Dauben Water Lilly) (ID 26717)". Erowid.org. Sep 10, 2003. erowid.org/exp/26717

 
DOSE: 3 flowers smoked Lotus/Lily - Nymphaea nouchali var caerulea (flowers)

BODY WEIGHT: 134 lb


I had always wanted a pond but never had the space. Well needless to say i found the space and began to look for a water lilly. having heard about the egyptian blue i proceded to look for one. i came to find out it's rather rare. but as i was at one aquatic store i came acrost a tropical hybrid that looked rather familiar. Dauben it was called. I had a hunch it was related just by the way it looked. to my suprise i was right. It's parents were Nymphea caerulea (egyptian blue) and micrantha. So i figured at the worst i'd have to double dosages being half the plant. I got about 3 flowers worth of petals and made a joint out of them.

Almost like weed but it dosn't make me as lethargic. Very light, relaxing and indeed a good sleep aid. I'd highly recomend this to pot as there is no side effects i noticed as the week went by. the smoke is harsh so a bong would be a good idea. onset was about 15 min and lasts for a few hours. I'm going to experiment more as my plant makes more flowers.
