It's Not Just My Mouth That's Numb
Cocaine

Citation:   Banjo. "It's Not Just My Mouth That's Numb: experience with Cocaine (ID 56077)". Erowid.org. Sep 21, 2006. erowid.org/exp/56077

 
DOSE:     Cocaine

BODY WEIGHT: 60 kg


About two months ago, I was forced to abrubtly end a two-month-old daily cocaine habit. Minor psychological withdrawals aside, that wasn't too bad. It's only after a few weeks that I've noticed that I may have done something awful to my mind.

I am numbed. A few of my emotions are intact, but even those are wild, fluctuating, unpredictable. I cannot at all, though, feel any real attachment to people. I am far more introverted. Despite attempts, I cannot seem to really care, or more severely, I cannot seem to really love anything or anyone.

Perhaps I did too much.
