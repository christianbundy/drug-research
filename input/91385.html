Wrong I Was
Morning Glory
by xlegal
Citation:   xlegal. "Wrong I Was: experience with Morning Glory (ID 91385)". Erowid.org. Jul 26, 2011. erowid.org/exp/91385

 
DOSE: 600 seeds oral Morning Glory (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 190 lb


So I bought a gigantic bag of heavenly blue morning glory seeds and thought that all the information I read was enough to try them (wrong I was). So I crushed up about 600 seeds counting them one by one to ensure the exact dose. I mixed it with a chocolate milkshake, didn't taste half bad.

Several hours went by with absolutely no effect, so the next day I wake up and my body just feels weird, feeling my face felt like I was touching another person. Its been 5 days since then and my body has yet to surpass the numb feeling. I feel like a fucking idiot, ruining my nurves just for a quick cheap trip?
