Intense Psychedlia
2C-P
by Knock
Citation:   Knock. "Intense Psychedlia: experience with 2C-P (ID 83066)". Erowid.org. Jan 21, 2010. erowid.org/exp/83066

 
DOSE: 14 mg oral 2C-P (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 56 kg


My setting for this trip was my house and I was on my own. Previous experience with psychs include lsd, mushrooms and 2c-e. my goal was to enjoy life for the next 10-16 hours.

T+00:00 Ingested 14mg 2c-p dissolved in water.
T+01:30 First visual distortions noticed.
T+02:30 Prevelant OEVs, some colours; pinks and greens.
T+03:00 My whole body is positively electrified, heavey OEVs, approaching full effect now I'm in my bedroom swaying back and forward and the whole room is moving with me. I go downstairs, turn on the tv and peoples faces are melting...turn off the tv.

For the next 6 hours I am pretty much incapacitated by intense psychedlia. I keep telling myself not to downplay this experience and then I would tell myself not to overplay it. I have never been so overwhelmed by a drug. I attribute the intensity of the experience to the bodily sensations as much as the hallucinations. I am almost constantly hit with intense rushes of trippieness.

At one point the hallucinations were pretty scary and I found myself thinking I was going to fall into a 'bad trip'. I never thought I could have a bad trip because its never the trip thats bad its the state of mind. I was ok, I wasnt having a bad trip but I felt I could lose my head if I wasnt careful.

T+09:00 The intensity is finally subsiding. Over the next 6-7 hours the visuals wind down.

T+16:00 Still slightly tripping. My face and the top of my chest are still electrified. Still getting occasional body rushes. This drug is overstaying its welcome. For the next 4 hours I have an annoying tension in my face as though Its the last place for the drug to leave my body.

T+20:00 I pass the 20 hour mark and I can still feel the twang of chemical in my body but I eventually fall into a restless sleep.

I got up after about 5 hours sleep still worn out from the experience. I was looking for a bit of intensity with this trip when I decided to take 14mg but I got more than I bargained for. Would I take this substance again? Not in a hurry but if I was I would definately not take this much.
