Light Doses of Datura as a Dream Aid
Datura Inoxia
by Trippernaut
Citation:   Trippernaut. "Light Doses of Datura as a Dream Aid: experience with Datura Inoxia (ID 17738)". Erowid.org. Sep 29, 2002. erowid.org/exp/17738

 
DOSE: 0.5 flowers oral Datura (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 145 lb


Recently, I was at the dentist, and I was going to call somone to give me a ride home. But I decided to walk home for some reason, despite it being a pretty long walk. During that walk home, I stumbled across a beautiful Datura Inoxia plant. I immediatly picked a thorn apple and a few flowers.

I tried smoking some Datura flower with Cannabis. At First, I noticed nothing but the Cannabis high. Then as I walked around, I relised that I did feel different, when I walked, my body moved a little more drunkenly, and colors seemed alot brighter, especially the sunset, and plants. I also seemed to have an extra mood lift that made me want to act strange, or laugh.

Then just before bed, I boiled a half a flower in a cup of water. It wasn't very bad tasting at all.

What an amazing night of sleep, I had tons of dreams, some I don't remember, others I do. And I remember several times during the night, somone opening my door and walking into my room, but apparently, noone ever opened my door. Most of the dreams I had took place in my house, so maybe it was an OBE.

Extremely Light Doses of Datura before bed are perfect for inducing lucid dreams or OBE's. I was amazed at the results I got.

I've read that some people wake up in the morning with a Datura hangover, and just feel like crap. But I guess that's only when you take high doses, cause I felt fine this morning. In fact, I feel great this morning.

Just remember don't take too much, cause it can kill you.
