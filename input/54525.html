Very Unenergetic
DXM & Buproprion (Wellbutrin)
by PsycheDevil
Citation:   PsycheDevil. "Very Unenergetic: experience with DXM & Buproprion (Wellbutrin) (ID 54525)". Erowid.org. Dec 19, 2007. erowid.org/exp/54525

 
DOSE: 300 mg oral Pharms - Bupropion (daily)
  300 mg oral DXM  

BODY WEIGHT: 250 lb


I previously had used DXM and Effexor (venlafaxine) together without incident, so I assumed that taking DXM and Wellbutrin (buproprion) together would go much the same. In my particular case, the positive aspects of the DXM experience itself were diminished completely, and I felt very unenergetic. In fact, I continued to feel fatigued for several days after ingesting DXM. For almost an entire week, I wanted to do nothing but rest in bed, getting up only to eat or use the restroom.

Thankfully, I was in between school semesters when this happened, and I work only when school is in session. There would have been no way I could have functioned either at work or at school. I fully recovered after about a week, but I'm lucky that in my ignorance I did not end up seriously hurt or dead. I once again was taught that I never can be too informed about any of the drugs I choose to take, especially regarding drug interactions.
