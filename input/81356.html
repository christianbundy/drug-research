DOSE: 200 mg   Modafinil

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


A 200mg dose of Modafinil produces the following effects in me:

- A non-euphoric rush of mental energy that lasts 20-30 minutes
- Muscular tension: jaw clenching, tightening of the chest
- Mental silence
- Anxiety (Im an anxious person by nature)
- Mild anhedonia that lasts up to a day

Modafinil begins to kick-in within 30 minutes and peaks at 80 minutes.

The effects are like Adderall, except without the euphoria.

Im a 21 year old male, 150 pounds, have a full tolerance to amphetamines, and am generally hypersensitive to psychoactives.
