DOSE:   repeated   Barbiturates - Phenobarbital

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I wouldnt say I get a buzz off Phenobarbital, or any schedule IV downers for that matter (unless Im smoking herb). Its more of a calm. It makes me more likely to fall asleep than Xanax, and like all schedule IVs, I dont like the feeling of the set in. I feel incoherent, confused, and drowsy. I find them much more enjoyable if I build a tolerance by taking a small dose a few hours before the real one. Or I take a medium dose and fall asleep on a night I dont have to work in the morning. The next day I take a large dose throughout the day and Im ready to have fun. These are the only drugs I do that with.

I have to say benzos are a lot better, although memory formation is much worse with them. Phenobarbital never made me forget a whole summer like benzos did.
There is clarity present with pheno that isnt there with benzos.

Phenobarbital at 300 or 400 mg makes me feel kind of drunk, but more coherent and apathetic. A lot of dragging the limbs and leaning against things goes on. Nothing close to Schedule II barbs from what I hear.
