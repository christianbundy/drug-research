Inspirational Journey (Poem)
Mushrooms
by Spike-KG
Citation:   Spike-KG. "Inspirational Journey (Poem): experience with Mushrooms (ID 10257)". Erowid.org. Jan 12, 2002. erowid.org/exp/10257

 
DOSE: 4.0 g oral Mushrooms

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


Back from the grave,
Reborn again.
Dont know how to behave,
Dont know how it began.

In total confusion,
Where I lay.
Lifes an illusion,
A game I play.

Dazed and confused,
I look at the ceiling.
My minds been abused,
But I just love the feeling.

The ceiling is twirling,
The walls only pulse.
The carpet is curling,
But I know its all false.

Stuck in a world,
Parallel to mine.
The colors all swirled,
A vision benign.

I roam around,
Unable to speak.
Falling off the ground,
As I reach my peak.
