Not for Opioid Withdrawal!
Trazodone
by Euslace Nodd
Citation:   Euslace Nodd. "Not for Opioid Withdrawal!: experience with Trazodone (ID 80597)". Erowid.org. Aug 21, 2009. erowid.org/exp/80597

 
DOSE: 75 mg   Pharms - Trazodone

BODY WEIGHT: 130 lb


I was coming off heroin one night and I knew it would get bad late at night and wake me up, so I took 75mg of Trazodone to knock me out. I woke up some hours later totally disoriented and sweaty. I walked into the bathroom and collapsed. It was like the most intense head-rush you can possibly have, I couldn't even see. I layed in the bathroom naked for some more hours until I felt better. I'll never use that stuff again.
