Poor for Recreation
Mirtazapine (Remeron)
by L-Train
Citation:   L-Train. "Poor for Recreation: experience with Mirtazapine (Remeron) (ID 51057)". Erowid.org. Jun 11, 2006. erowid.org/exp/51057

 
DOSE: 45 mg oral Pharms - Mirtazapine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


I've tried a number of pills for their recreational effects, such as Vicodin, Ultracet, Klonopin, Xanax, and even Benadryl. I got a hold of some Mirtazapine and decided to give it a try.

I ingested one 45mg pill at around 10pm and waited for the effects. I remember going to the bathroom and the room shifted colors to a gold color. This may have been placebo, but I dont know. When the pill kicked in it was very mellow, but too mellow for me. I thought that my breathing was becoming more and more shallow as time progressed. I got a semi-nice buzz from it. The only thing I didnt like was the buzzing in my joints, from there I laid down and drifted off to sleep.
