No Effects Whatsoever
Galangal
by Pebbles
Citation:   Pebbles. "No Effects Whatsoever: experience with Galangal (ID 98837)". Erowid.org. Jan 7, 2013. erowid.org/exp/98837

 
DOSE: 2 oz oral Galangal (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 115 lb


I brewed some tea with 2oz of galangal root following others' recipes. I ground it up in a coffee grinder and steeped the powdered root it in a french press with 2c boiled water for 15min. I consumed it all at once and experienced no effects. Nothing after 30min, 2hrs later....still nothing. No warmth, no visuals, nothing happened.
