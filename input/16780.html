Harsh Smoke, Definite Buzz
Leonotis leonurus (Wild Dagga)
by Skandre
Citation:   Skandre. "Harsh Smoke, Definite Buzz: experience with Leonotis leonurus (Wild Dagga) (ID 16780)". Erowid.org. Aug 17, 2002. erowid.org/exp/16780

 
DOSE: 1.0 g smoked Leonotis leonurus (flowers)

BODY WEIGHT: 185 lb


I recently recieved an ounce of Wild Dagga from an online vendor. I had read some interesting reports on the substance online, and was anxious to see what effects smoking the plant produced.

Working with the substance is a bit difficult...like its cousin, marihuanilla, the plant has small 'thorns' within the flowers. With a little time I was able to pack a nice sized bowl. Unfortunately, the smoke does not taste very pleasant, and it is somewhat harsh on the lungs. It may be better to use a water pipe with this substance. On the bright side, I noticed a definite buzz. It sets in very quickly, and is similar to a marijuana buzz, but much milder with some opiate effects. It did seem difficult to sustain the buzz after a while.

In conclusion, I think this plant is a much better option than Marihuanilla. It has stronger effects and is a little easier to smoke. I would recommend smoking Wild Dagga with a water pipe. I also noticed that there is a resin extract available. Next time, I will order the extract.
