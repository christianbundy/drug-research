Long Term Use
GBL
by Jolina Bloggs
Citation:   Jolina Bloggs. "Long Term Use: experience with GBL (ID 74434)". Erowid.org. Oct 15, 2008. erowid.org/exp/74434

 
DOSE:   repeated oral GBL (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 126 lb


My experience with GBL use started about a year ago. Started off as recreational, but had other health issues which I used GBL to counteract. Soon it turned into round the clock use, taking in excess of 2ml every one and a half hours to the point where I was wracked with anxiety and depression both on and when trying to withdraw. I feel like I'm on the brink of a nervouse breakdown. All my internal organs ache. I think I'm poisoning myself. Have tried to seek help but nobody knows what I'm talking about. Have found similar reports of usage on the net, but they're all abroad.
