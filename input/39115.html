Entering a World of Death
Fentanyl
by Big Steve
Citation:   Big Steve. "Entering a World of Death: experience with Fentanyl (ID 39115)". Erowid.org. Jan 4, 2005. erowid.org/exp/39115

 
DOSE: 50 ug buccal Pharms - Fentanyl (patch)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I have used oxycontin and other strong opiates that like before, but I have never really respected opiates as much as when I tried Fentanyl.

My night started off with chewing the 50 microgram patch and I just waited for it to kick in. I wasn't fully aware of the danger I would take when chewing the patch and getting the full dosage all at once. I started feeling really good around 5 minutes and I decided it was best if I didn't drive any longer. I tried stepping out of the car and I fell on my face, not being able to use my muscles or anything.

The next thing I realize is that I'm in the ER with a tube down my throat and I learned that my body stopped breathing and my body was without oxygen for 3 minutes. I would not suggest trying Fentanyl unless you are smart and cautious in doing so. Fentanyl is not a drug to play around with. I
