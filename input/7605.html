Bunk unless already tripping
Damiana
by Burnvictim
Citation:   Burnvictim. "Bunk unless already tripping: experience with Damiana (ID 7605)". Erowid.org. Jun 24, 2001. erowid.org/exp/7605

 
DOSE: 1 cig. smoked Damiana

BODY WEIGHT: 210 lb


I ordered some Damiana so I could get a legal buzz. I was disappointed in the effects. My expectations were not that high, but I hoped to feel something of substance. The texture was nice for rolling a joint and the taste was pretty good, kinda flowery. However, there was no effect. I used one friend as a follow up test group. He felt nothing. This substance will disappoint those who are looking for a legal marijuana type high. It is not bad as a cigarette substitute.

One note however, I gave another drag to the same friend who was coming down off morning glory seeds and this time he got a buzz off it. Said it kinda revived the LSA feeling stronger. Gave some to the friend a third time when he was sober and no effects. In conclusion, if you think this stuff will get you high, think again ( unless maybe if you take it with pschodelics).
