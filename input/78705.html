Treats Withdrawal
Quetiapine (Seroquel)
by Durandal
Citation:   Durandal. "Treats Withdrawal: experience with Quetiapine (Seroquel) (ID 78705)". Erowid.org. May 12, 2009. erowid.org/exp/78705

 
DOSE:   repeated oral Pharms - Quetiapine

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


I had developed an oxy and hydro habit, diagnosed bipolar II, and also smoking up a storm (cigarettes). Started on Seroquel for sleep problems when I was trying to score benzos. I now regret that.

I had to quit, and doing it cold turkey is technically the fastest way but the most painful. My legs felt like they were on fire, overwhelmed the feeling in my other muscles, and I couldn't sleep for hours. Took the Seroquel, and I was asleep.

Next morning I was awake and while I still ached, the anxiety, the pains, especially in my legs was relieved. Cravings were reduced, and I started taking little bites of the 50mg pills a day, careful not to exceed 150mg a day. The insomnia totally vanished and I started having these dreams that were so powerful I could swear they were real.

Quitting is never a nice experience but Seroquel definitely helps with withdrawal symptoms.
