Not Good Enough to Repeat
Vinpocetine
by DjCherny
Citation:   DjCherny. "Not Good Enough to Repeat: experience with Vinpocetine (ID 33567)". Erowid.org. Jun 13, 2007. erowid.org/exp/33567

 
DOSE: 10 mg oral Vinpocetine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


I have tried Vinpocetine on a few occasions. I have tried both 5 mg and 10 mg doses. I have tried it on an empty stomach, and with a meal. Every time that I have taken it, I just haven't been able to grasp any positive effects.

I have heard that Vinpocetine is good at increasing blood flow to the brain, and that it can increase concentration. I may feel the increase in blood flow, but it doesn't necessarily feel good to me. When I take this it makes me feel dizzy, like I'm about to fall forward over myself. This feeling is not overwhelming, but is somewhat constant.

I have much experience with smart drugs/nutrients including Hydergine, Acetyl-L-Carnitine, DMAE, P. Choline, and daily dosages of Piracetam. I enjoy all of them, except for DMAE. Vinpocetine is not something I will continue to take.
