Put Me to Sleep
Poppy - California
by Riconoen
Citation:   Riconoen. "Put Me to Sleep: experience with Poppy - California (ID 53246)". Erowid.org. Apr 1, 2007. erowid.org/exp/53246

 
DOSE: 4 flowers sublingual Poppies - California (flowers)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


Well I heard that chewing california poppy petals will get you high so I decided 'what the hell' and I picked four petals off the plants that were growing in my front yard and chewed them like gum. I immediatly felt a wave of relaxation and drowsiness come over me. After about ten minutes of chewing the petals I really felt like going to sleep. About ten minues later it wore off and I had a general feeling of drowsiness the rest of the day. Do all in all it's good for insomnia but not much else.
