Rode the Blazing Heat
Capsicum spp. (Serrano)
by al quimica
Citation:   al quimica. "Rode the Blazing Heat: experience with Capsicum spp. (Serrano) (ID 62929)". Erowid.org. May 23, 2007. erowid.org/exp/62929

 
DOSE: 2 oral Capsicum spp.

BODY WEIGHT: 155 lb


Quickly chewed up 2 peppers (Serranos).

Sweated like mad, and had tingling all over my skin. Once the pain peaked, the coasting began, with a sense of increased focus and slight euphoria. Definitely something there, and it feels great on a really hot day when sweat just douses me.
