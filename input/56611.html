To Close to the Screen at Movies
Modafinil
by kidkodine
Citation:   kidkodine. "To Close to the Screen at Movies: experience with Modafinil (ID 56611)". Erowid.org. Feb 7, 2007. erowid.org/exp/56611

 
DOSE: 200 mg oral Modafinil (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


Just my observation. I got my Doctor to suscribe after pestering him. I do overnight yacht racing and find it hard to stay alert. I thought that Modafil would fit the bill.

Took 200mg during the day at 3pm. Felt baseline with a little anxiety as I did not know what to expect. At 10pm still felt baseline. Went to bed at 12am, still baseline but a little tired.

All night I had the most intense dreams. Very uncomfortable. Even had audio, texture - just too much. Maybe I will test again in an all nighter, but going to sleep on the stuff is a little too much for me.
