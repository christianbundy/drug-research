Nothing But a Headache
Sceletium tortuosum (Kanna)
by Cielo
Citation:   Cielo. "Nothing But a Headache: experience with Sceletium tortuosum (Kanna) (ID 80227)". Erowid.org. Jul 28, 2009. erowid.org/exp/80227

 
DOSE: 400 mg oral Sceletium tortuosum (flowers)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


I had read a wide variety of reports about the effects of Kanna (which were reportedly positive for the most part) and decided to give it whirl. I was working in my kitchen at the time listing to some music and took about 1/3 to 1/2 gram of the dried flowers orally. I let them sit in my mouth for a few minutes until they disintegrated and proceeded to swallow. This was at about 11 am. Over the next hour my energy level started to tank and a strong headache soon followed. The music eventually became unbearable as it made the headache worse. I forgot that I had even taken the Kanna but around 4:30 pm, I remembered when I finally put two and two together and recalled that I had read somewhere that Kanna can cause headaches. No positive effects were noticed at all. Needless to say, I won't be taking Kanna ever again.
