Overall review:

A fun drug. Definitely has party potential  especially the initial hits  very euphoric; almost just like rolling. Coulda been passed this off as bunk molly in the past actually; thinking about it. However, it had a very distinct appearance. White clumpy powder; very clumpy when grinding to snort as well, had to keep cutting it up a bunch of times.

Dosing past 60 mgs for me was way more speedy/amphetamine-like. Also, after oral dosing, followed by intranasal dosing, the blurred vision got to be rather intense! Too intense at some points; I could barely look straight ahead without my vision fuzzing. But if I looked side to side or more rapidly, I could focus. Furthermore, I had zero libido on this stuff. No sex drive whatsoever.

Overall, substance gets an 8/10 in my books. Definitely fun. Tiny hangover... nothing compared to other stimulants, easily cured by some cannabis or synthetic noids :).
