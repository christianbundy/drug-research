DOSE: 75 seeds oral Turbina corymbosa (seeds)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


After extensive research about rivea, and extensive experience with LSD, mushrooms, chlorinated inhalents, and other various hallucinagens, aquired 100 seeds of this obscure morning glory cousin. I took 75 to feel out the dosage, and got a mellow body high for about 4 hours. The seeds are tiny compared to heavenly blue, but about the same # per dose.
