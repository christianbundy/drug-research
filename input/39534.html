DOSE:   repeated   MDMA

BODY WEIGHT: 68 kg


I've been taking MDMA (and LSD, ketamine, mushrooms, weed, cocaine) for about 5 years now. I took 800mg in 8 hours and I had no problem. For the past (about) 9 months I've been having serious visual distortions (I cant focus right, I'm seeing weird things, many times I see everything moving slow etc.).

I havent continued taking any drug since then but my vision is totally wrong. I went to a doctor (one of the best ones in my country) to see my eyes and he said i was ok. But sure thing is that I'm no ok. It's been six months and I cant see anything right.
