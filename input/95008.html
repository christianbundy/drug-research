Amazing Substance
25I-NBOMe
by ResearchingChemicals
Citation:   ResearchingChemicals. "Amazing Substance: experience with 25I-NBOMe (ID 95008)". Erowid.org. Mar 1, 2012. erowid.org/exp/95008

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 2 hits oral 2C-I-NBOMe (blotter / tab)
  T+ 6:00 2 lines insufflated 2C-I-NBOMe (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 70 kg


First I decided to take to blotters to test it out and I had read earlier that 2-3mg is a really strong dose.

T+00.00 I took the 2 blotter and put them between my gums.

T+00.15 Now some effects are taking place and I feel relaxed and everything looks a bit sharper yet soft in a way, a few visuals but not much at all.

T+01.00 Now its starting to take effect but still not as strong as I would hope, thinking about re-dosing but then I decide to wait.

T+06.00 I skip to 6 hours after I take them because there was not much to say about those 2 mg, maybe I have some kind of natural tolerance or it could be the result of cross tolerance with something else I have been taking. I decide to redose, I tip then of the bag of powder (25i-nbome hcl) to the glass table. I make 2 lines (no scale). I read also that it works allot better through the nose. I stick a note in my nose and niff the line, and of course the lines were a bit too close to each other or then I'm just sniffing like Henry the hoover, both lines go up my nose. No pain though, unlike 2C-I and all the other 2Cs.

A bit of panic as I don't know if I just took way too much or not but I calm my self down and listen to music, which sounds so fucking nice on this drug.

+01.00 From re-dose

Wow! visuals are crazy and the music is intense, waves of 3D objects have taken over my living room and everything looks beautiful! This is amazing, on a whole other level than any 2C, bromo, doc, mushrooms, I feel great and no worries in the world!

If any one is planning on taking this then sniff it, its meant to sniff!

+2.00 These are really the most amazing visuals I have ever gotten from psychedelic (DMT not included and I haven't ever tried ayahuasca) even 'cooler' than on high doses of LSD. Music sounds better than on 1200µg of LSD!

Amazing party drug! I don't feel very stimulated even though this is a psychedelic stimulant? But euphoria I feel quite allot and this is a really social drug although on high doses its a bit hard to have a real conversation :D If you know what I mean (and I don't mean I was mumbling and couldn't get my words out)

Not one bad thing to say about this drug at all!

Its a real improvement to any 2C because on this I feel so good coming up instead of feeling sick and uncomfortable, and the good feeling stays with me throughout the trip, perfection!
