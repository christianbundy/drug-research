A Pleasant Afternoon
P. cyanescens, Ephedrine & Caffeine
by Rice Paper
Citation:   Rice Paper. "A Pleasant Afternoon: experience with P. cyanescens, Ephedrine & Caffeine (ID 23614)". Erowid.org. Feb 21, 2006. erowid.org/exp/23614

 
DOSE: 1.5 g oral Mushrooms - P. cyanescens  
  50 mg oral Ephedrine (pill / tablet)
  400 ug oral Caffeine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb


This proved to be a very pleasant mix.

1.5g of P Cayanescens Mushrooms
2 Yellow Jackets [each contains 25mg ephedra alkaloids and 200mg caffeine alkaloids]

T + 00:00 I eat 1.5 grams of the mushrooms, and swollow the two yellow jackets

T + 00:10 I feel very good, euphoria.

T + 00:30 Euphoria has grown quite a bit, beginning to get some visual distortions, with objects stretching and bending a bit.

T + 01:00 Experience peaks. Good closed eye visuals, no nausea, feel very very good about myself, feel very energized and a very strong euphoria.

Peak lasts for about 3 and a half hours.

Coming down - the effects simply trail off. No negative after effects whatsoever.

Afterward, I get a good nights sleep and wake up the next morning feeling wonderful.

Provided for a very pleasant experience lasting about 4 to 5 hours with no negative side effects experienced.
