Craving, Trouble Breathing
Products - Bath Salts, Plant Food, etc. ('White Horse')
by mrseriousbiz
Citation:   mrseriousbiz. "Craving, Trouble Breathing: experience with Products - Bath Salts, Plant Food, etc. ('White Horse') (ID 91209)". Erowid.org. Aug 30, 2011. erowid.org/exp/91209

 
DOSE:   repeated insufflated Products - Bath Salts, Plant Food, etc (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


[Erowid Note: 'Bath salts' and similar products generally do not list ingredients or dosage on their packaging. Analysis has revealed that ingredients in a single product of this type may vary over time.]

Started out with small lines about 8 pm. It had a similar effect to coke but way more haunting. Noticed that my balance was off and I started losing focus. Into fifth hour I started to enjoy the powerful high. At 5 am finished last line and began to crave more. The drug made me extremely paranoid, off balance, drool and the crash was horrible. I had to concentrate hard just to speak. I craved the drug so much the next day, I drove to get more knowing I barely could control my car. [Erowid Note: Driving while intoxicated or tripping is dangerous and irresponsible because it endangers other people. Don't do it!]

Day two was a better experience but this time my crash ended with trouble breathing, my heart beating out of my chest, and pains I'm my muscles. When I used the bathroom my urine was dark. I began to panic as I felt as if I was slipping away. At this point I had a family friend drive me to the emergency room. This drug landed me a three day hospital stay with kidney and liver damage. I do not recommend bath salts.

[Reported Dose: '500 mg in 2 day span 2 packs']
