Smoking vs Eating Mushrooms
Mushrooms (smoked)
by itch
Citation:   itch. "Smoking vs Eating Mushrooms: experience with Mushrooms (smoked) (ID 9160)". Erowid.org. Oct 24, 2001. erowid.org/exp/9160

 
DOSE:   smoked Mushrooms

BODY WEIGHT: 135 lb


The first time i tried mushrooms i did the traditional method of chewing them and holding them in your mouth. I ate somewhere around 2.5 grams of dried mushrooms. I found the experance very enjoyable. With intense images and thoughts when my eyes where closed to mild open eye visuals. The whole experaince lasted somewhere around 5 hours.

The next time i tried muchrooms i tried smoking them. i smoked a large quantity and began to feel the same beginning effects as before except this time i didnt wait 30 minutes to feel effects they were damn near instant. I was feeling early begingings of a good trip i.e. trails breathing walls etc. I felt the effects become slightly more intense before quickly wearing off. All effects had worn of in less than 30 minutes.

In conclusion i would say that eating the mushrooms is much more effective, is much longer and more intense.
