Heart Palpitations Days After Overdose
5-HTP
by aburgz123
Citation:   aburgz123. "Heart Palpitations Days After Overdose: experience with 5-HTP (ID 91615)". Erowid.org. Jul 26, 2011. erowid.org/exp/91615

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 50 mg oral Tryptophan - 5-HTP (pill / tablet)
  T+ 2:00 50 mg oral Tryptophan - 5-HTP (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 135 lb


Well I was in a bad mood in the morning so I decided to take 50mg (half a pill) of 5-HTP by mixing it in a sprite soda and drinking it. About an hour later my mood got significantly better. About two hours later being the impulsive idiot that I am I decided to have more. I mixed up another 50mg (the remaining pill) and drank it down with another Sprite. About 45 minutes later my face started getting extremely hot. I first disregarded it as nothing until I felt my blood pressure rise like never before. It felt like my head was being stuffed full of paper. Then my heart started beating faster then I have ever felt it causing the worst palpitations.

Basically I got random strikes of high blood pressure and palpitations during the days which stop after 3 days.

I got extremely worried I was going to have a heart attack and felt chest pain. Always read up on anything you take and just because it's an amino acid doesn't make it a free ride.
