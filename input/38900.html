Unremarkable
Piperazines ('6-triflouro-N-benzyl-methyl piperazine')
by Cactushead
Citation:   Cactushead. "Unremarkable: experience with Piperazines ('6-triflouro-N-benzyl-methyl piperazine') (ID 38900)". Erowid.org. Nov 3, 2005. erowid.org/exp/38900

 
DOSE: 1 tablet oral Piperazines

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb


11:30- One down the hatch.
12:30- Feeling a little warm. A little cramped in my left arm and slight tension in head.

1:30- Some slight energy and body warmth. My left arm is very hot indeed. My body is comfortable other than a very slight tension in my head. There are some slight changes in how movement looks. Perhaps a little more jagged, or fragmented than usual. Other than these effects, Im noticing little else.

2:00- Feeling pretty relaxed with a slight headache. As I watch the words on the screen, I am noticing very slight movement as well.

3:00- Slight headache still. The experience was overall very unremarkable.
