Jumping Sky
4-Ho-DiPT, Alcohol, & Cannabis
by Jackle
Citation:   Jackle. "Jumping Sky: experience with 4-Ho-DiPT, Alcohol, & Cannabis (ID 27544)". Erowid.org. Oct 13, 2003. erowid.org/exp/27544

 
DOSE: 5 glasses oral Alcohol - Hard
  20 mg oral 4-HO-DiPT
  3 oral Alcohol - Beer/Wine
  2 joints/cigs smoked Cannabis

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


I had aquired exactly 30 mg of 4-HO-DiPT (as a white powder) and divided it up by eye into three piles of ~10 mg each. I was very precise with this, so I am pretty confident that the piles were equal. Also, I knew it probably wouldn't hurt if I was off by a mg or two with this drug, so dividing by eye was prefered.

The night began with drinking at a bar before the 4-HO came into play. I was happy to see friends I hadn't seen in a long time. I drank 'trash can punch' which I can only assume had everclear in it ...perhaps vodka? Anyway, I had 5 cups of that. Then, as a group, we went to a party out in a field, basically. I opened a beer, got out two doses of 10 mg each of 4-HO-DiPT, and dropped them both in my beer. It tasted nasty, but after a while it dissolved a little more and I couldn't taste it. I was almost drunk at this point. I had some more beers, and smoked 2 joints of some really good bud with several other people before I felt anything from the 4-HO. I do not know how long it took to come on or how long I tripped for. (It was more than 2 hours before the trip was over - that is safe to say).

I was being more talkative (overly so) and had more energy. The bad part is my memory gets a little hazy about the time I started tripping, so I don't really know what happened next, but I do remember stumbling around in circles just 'tripping balls' as they say. I kept drinking the whole night as well. I stretched back very far (while standing) to look at the sky several times. I looked like someone painted the sky there and it seemed to be jumping around. The clouds made trails. Everything seemed to be moving. I couldn't get over how surreal the sky looked. I remember closing my eyes and seeing green and purple crystals everywhere. They were shiny. The longer I looked, the more 'crystals' grew and filled up my closed-eyed visual field. I had a very fun night (I know that much), but once again, I was too drunk to remember a lot of it.

When I woke up the next day, I had scabs from minor cuts/scrapes on my knee, hand and face for reasons unknown to me. I guess I fell down or tripped a few times. It was pretty dark out there.
