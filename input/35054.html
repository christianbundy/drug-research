Catnip
by Dan the Dirtbag
Citation:   Dan the Dirtbag. "For Those Bored with Pot: experience with Catnip (ID 35054)". Erowid.org. Jul 11, 2004. erowid.org/exp/35054

 
DOSE: 3 joints/cigs smoked Catnip (leaves)

BODY WEIGHT: 165 lb


Smoked 3 rolled cigarettes of catnip over an hour or so. Felt a rush of calm/chill after two or three drags. Wandered around on a golf course in the rain for awhile smoking the other 2 cigs. Whole trip was similiar to a mild pot buzz, without the apathy (which is a bonus for me. The buzz wears off soon after you finish smoking, and leaves you content and drowsy (hyper-chill as I call it). All in all, not something for the gutterrat drug fiend who constantly strives to trip balls, but a good way to chill for those who are bored/disillusioned with getting stupid on pot.
