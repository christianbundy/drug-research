Quick Sickness
Modafinil & Methadone
by KF
Citation:   KF. "Quick Sickness: experience with Modafinil & Methadone (ID 34028)". Erowid.org. May 31, 2004. erowid.org/exp/34028

 
DOSE:   oral Methadone
  1000 mg oral Modafinil


I can assure you that even a light dose of 200mg caused my methadone to be metabolized very quickly. This is, of course subjective, as I didn't do blood levels with and without. I loved the feeling of modafanil and would try to use it to stay awake and feel euphoric, which doses of 1000mg to 1500mg do.

But I had take supplemental methadone every few hours just to avoid withdrawal sickness, which with methadone-done doesnt come on heavy for 48 hours or longer. A single large provigil dose causes the sickness to come on with a startlingly fast speed.
