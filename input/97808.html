Just Chillin' Out
Wild Lettuce & Passion Flower (Tinctures)
by Rocky
Citation:   Rocky. "Just Chillin' Out: experience with Wild Lettuce & Passion Flower (Tinctures) (ID 97808)". Erowid.org. Oct 2, 2012. erowid.org/exp/97808

 
DOSE: 30 drops oral Lactuca - L. canadensis (liquid)
  30 drops oral Passion Flower (liquid)

BODY WEIGHT: 105 lb


It was a nice saturday afternoon around 4 o clock and I had some wild lettuce tincture and passion flower tincture. I mixed both in a little water and downed it. The effects occured in about 20 minutes. The effect was medium and very pleasant, all of a sudden I felt giddy and euphoric. Around a minute later I felt num and floaty. The entire thing lasted around 3 hours. It was not a very physical buzz/high but it was great. No loss of memory or bad side effects just a little bit sleepy. No hangover effect when it ended just relaxed and calm. In general the whole thing was great and I found it to be a great combination of herbs.
