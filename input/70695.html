Disturbed
Salvia Divinorum (21x extract)
by Gene
Citation:   Gene. "Disturbed: experience with Salvia Divinorum (21x extract) (ID 70695)". Erowid.org. Apr 21, 2008. erowid.org/exp/70695

 
DOSE:   smoked Salvia divinorum (extract)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


Have you ever felt like the walls were breathing while looking at them? Have you ever felt you could look at the T.V. as if you were looking through a window into a new world? I have, after I smoked Salvia for the second time. It left me emotionally disturbed, and a feeling as if I have a speech impediment. I can think the words but they come out wrong. The first time I did salvia I'll admit, it was the best thing that could ever happen to me. I had a wonderful time, though I walked around when I shouldn't have. I have had these persistent visual disturbances for just about 2-3 weeks. I've had bad memory but that could also be due to marijuana because I smoked it habitually.
