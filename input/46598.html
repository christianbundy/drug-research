Interesting Combination
DXM & Valerian
by Mr. Asialo
Citation:   Mr. Asialo. "Interesting Combination: experience with DXM & Valerian (ID 46598)". Erowid.org. Jan 11, 2009. erowid.org/exp/46598

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 240 mg oral DXM (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:20 450 mg oral Valerian (capsule)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I'm starting to become immune to DxM, because of the supposed 50 dose rule, I guess. So, I took double the minimal dose in order to get a mild high. Since I was experimenting with the recreational/etcetera effects of valerian, I popped one pill of said Valerian. What was unexpected, however, was my trip which felt as heavy as when I took 240 mg doses with 0-tolerance and 0-immunity.

Further testing is needed, but I just wanted to share this with others.
