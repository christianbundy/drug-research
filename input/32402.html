Clearer Mind and More Energy
Ginkgo biloba
by Rummios
Citation:   Rummios. "Clearer Mind and More Energy: experience with Ginkgo biloba (ID 32402)". Erowid.org. Mar 6, 2007. erowid.org/exp/32402

 
DOSE: 1.0 g oral Ginkgo biloba (tea)
    smoked Ginkgo biloba (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


I bought some ginkgo foliage and started making tea from it. This is a great way to start the morning! My mind feels clearer and I feel that I can think faster and have more energy. There may even be some slight visual enhancement, but I'm not sure. Noticeable effects seem to last a couple hours, but the positive effects last for most of the day.
My usual dose is about 1g in 1 cup of water. Combine these in a pot, heat it up on the stove on high until boiling, then let it lightly boil on medium heat for about 5 minutes. Pour into a cup, filtering out the residue, which should leave a bit less than a cup of water. The taste is not too bad, though the taste gets stronger near the bottom of the cup.

As a side note, smoking the leaf (a hit or two from a water pipe) definitely has an effect, but I'm not sure what and it doesn't last long.
