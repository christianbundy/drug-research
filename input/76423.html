False Positive for Opiates
DXM & Drug Testing
by Abakua81
Citation:   Abakua81. "False Positive for Opiates: experience with DXM & Drug Testing (ID 76423)". Erowid.org. Jan 21, 2009. erowid.org/exp/76423

 
DOSE: 200 mg oral DXM

BODY WEIGHT: 220 lb


I tested positive for opiates the day after taking a DXM containing product. I took about 200mg of Dextromethorphan HBr over a 24 hour period for a cold and an EMIT test was positive for opiates the next morning. I had heard of it causing false positives when taken in recreational doses. What I took was hardly recreational and it still caused the positive. I am on probation so they sent the urine to a lab for a GC/MS confirmation. After 10 days of stressing out about going back to jail the test came back negative.
