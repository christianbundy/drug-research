Did Not Assist With Withdrawal Symptoms
5-HTP & Paroxetine (Paxil)
by Sleepdust
Citation:   Sleepdust. "Did Not Assist With Withdrawal Symptoms: experience with 5-HTP & Paroxetine (Paxil) (ID 71864)". Erowid.org. Jun 16, 2008. erowid.org/exp/71864

 
DOSE: 100 mg oral Tryptophan - 5-HTP (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 165 lb


After seven full days without Paxil (I took 40mg/day for five years), I couldn't stand the constant crying over nothing and the body shocks that are so well known by other people going through Paxil withdrawals, so I took 100 mg 5-HTP. After a couple of hours, I didn't notice any difference in my attitude, and my withdrawal symptoms were just as severe as before.
