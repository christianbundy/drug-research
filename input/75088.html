Works, But So Itchy!
Carisoprodol (Soma), Coffee, Alcohol & Cannabis
by Itchy
Citation:   Itchy. "Works, But So Itchy!: experience with Carisoprodol (Soma), Coffee, Alcohol & Cannabis (ID 75088)". Erowid.org. Nov 17, 2008. erowid.org/exp/75088

 
DOSE: 350 mg oral Pharms - Carisoprodol (pill / tablet)
    oral Coffee (liquid)
  1 glass oral Alcohol - Beer/Wine (liquid)
      Cannabis (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


I had had some Soma pills kicking around for years, and never took them, since recreational muscle relaxers are just not my cup o' tea. This time though, I was feeling a real twinge in my shoulder from my non-ergonomic computer setup, so I decided I would take one of the Somas. I took one 350mg pill, which I had in combination with plenty of coffee, 1 beer, and some weed.

Well, about 2 hours after I took it, I started feeling very itchy, especially in the armpits and groin. This morning I am still itchy, and I feel weak as if the effects are lingering. I am also noticably red, as if I have a mild sunburn all over my body. My verdict is that Soma is a very effective drug, but if are going to try it recreationaly, try a small dose first just to see if you are going to trigger an allergic reaction doing it - I shudder to think what might have occurred if I just popped 5 of them and chugged a beer for fun.
