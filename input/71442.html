Good Times
Ephedrine
by Sub
Citation:   Sub. "Good Times: experience with Ephedrine (ID 71442)". Erowid.org. Jan 9, 2009. erowid.org/exp/71442

 
DOSE: 25 mg oral Ephedrine (capsule)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


My first experience was by myself in my house. A friend gave me a pill to try out so on a Saturday I took it.

2pm-popped 1 ephedrine capsule with 25mg dose.

about 2:40pm I started to notice an elevated heart rate and a great sense of energy. Mouth is extremely dry and water doesn't last very long. My mind feels a bit cloudy.

3:00pm-I have a tremendous amount of productive energy. I have so much energy that I do all my chores and clean the entire house for no reason. My heart rate is much higher and I am sweating while sitting. When I close my eyes, I feel like I am floating. I feel so much stronger and capable of anything. I have little sense of pain and minor annoyances don't bug me. There is no way I would be able to read a book now.

6:00pm-Time passed quickly and it felt like only 15 minutes went by. My feet hurt for some reason. My energy level is down as well.

7:30pm-Extra boost is completely gone. I still have energy but nothing significant. I am going to sleep well tonight.

My conclusion about ephedra- A little bit of ephedra goes along way and makes me feel much better than being drunk.
