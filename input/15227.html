Long Night
2C-T-7, MDMA, GHB, Methamphetamine, & Ketamine
by Hank the Hustler
Citation:   Hank the Hustler. "Long Night: experience with 2C-T-7, MDMA, GHB, Methamphetamine, & Ketamine (ID 15227)". Erowid.org. Jun 6, 2002. erowid.org/exp/15227

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 2 tablets oral MDMA (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:00 2.0 ml oral GHB (liquid)
  T+ 1:00 1 tablet oral MDMA (pill / tablet)
  T+ 2:00 1 tablet oral MDMA (pill / tablet)
  T+ 2:00 2.0 ml oral GHB (liquid)
  T+ 4:00 1 tablet oral MDMA (pill / tablet)
  T+ 4:00 2.0 ml oral GHB (liquid)
  T+ 6:00 2.0 ml oral GHB (liquid)
  T+ 6:00 20 mg insufflated Methamphetamine (powder / crystals)
  T+ 6:30 25 mg oral 2C-T-7 (powder / crystals)
  T+ 9:30 30 mg insufflated Ketamine (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


It was my birthday. I was excited to be going out to one of my favorite dance clubs. I was very much in a great frame of mind; very happy and content.

I consider myself an experienced (about 7 years) user of MDMA. I have used GHB, Crystal Meth, and Ketamine for about 1 year. It was my third experience with 2C-T-7. The previous 2 experiences were very good, but they involved using 2CT7 without any other substances.

The night was going very well using MDMA and GHB. I was dancing alot and enjoying myself immensely. After the club closed, my friends and I went to an after party. There, I decided to do 2CT7. I had tried 2CT7 twice before by itself and had glowing experiences. I was curious to see how 2CT7 would be in combination with other drugs.

About 1 hour after I took the 2CT7 orally, I began to 'ramp up' and feel excited. At this point I began to see some visual effects and definite color enhancement. I had taken a dose of GHB approximately 30 minutes prior to taking the 2CT7. After about 2 hours of taking the 2CT7, I began to get slightly disoriented and extremely nauseated. I should note that I almost never vomit when taking drugs: until that moment. I proceeded to vomit and dry-heave for approximately 1 hour after that point.

I think my friends were getting bit concerned with me at that point and took me home. We then sat around my place for a few hours. I was well into the trip at that point. I was seeing visual effects such as tracers, closed-eye visuals, and color enhancement. I was still unable to keep anything in my stomach during this period.

I was feeling very agitated during this period also. I was getting a bit frightened because my pulse was getting a bit rapid and I was getting very dehydrated. I was also seeing images and hearing sounds that were not actually present. I was getting very tired at this point as it had been a long night of partying. I basically wanted the trip be over at this point, but I knew I was only about half-way though at this point. I just kept telling myself that I had taken a lot of drugs and eventually it would end.

My friends were snorting ketamine during this time, so I thought some K might help to relax me. I snorted about 5 bumps of K in about 30 minutes. The K changed the trip. It was hard to explain, but I was now hallucinating in pools. This was cool, but failed to relax me.

I decided at that point just to ride out the trip without any other drugs. Gradually, I was able to drink some water and my pulse became normal. About 8 hours after I took the 2CT7, I started to come down.

Overall, the experience wasn't all that bad; except for the nausea. I think the GHB in combination with the 2CT7 caused the vomiting. I definitely won't do those 2 in combination again. The ketamine mixed with 2CT7 was pretty cool, though.

I wouldn't recommend doing all of these drugs in combination. I think 2CT7 is a drug best enjoyed by itself (or maybe with some K) during a time when you can devote 12 hours to the experience.
