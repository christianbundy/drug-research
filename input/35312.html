In the Clear
DXM (with CPM) & Drug Testing
by Jay
Citation:   Jay. "In the Clear: experience with DXM (with CPM) & Drug Testing (ID 35312)". Erowid.org. Jun 22, 2007. erowid.org/exp/35312

 
DOSE: 420 mg oral DXM (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


I have had a few dxm experiencs with DXM prior to this but hadnt really 'tripped'. So I decided I would take 14 triple c's in order to achieve this. I dosed on a sunday and had a great experience. I won't go into details, bcuz it is not the subject. But the next day I found out I was going to have a drug test (the standard one) and was worried because I had read that DXM can sometimes cause false positives for PCP and/or opiates. I have never done any of that, so having to explain why I tested positive for either one of those to my parents would not be an easy task. A few days passed and I got my results, everything was negative, and what a relief that was.
