Wakes Me Up
Phentermine
by B
Citation:   B. "Wakes Me Up: experience with Phentermine (ID 67973)". Erowid.org. Dec 27, 2007. erowid.org/exp/67973

 
DOSE: 37.5 mg insufflated Pharms - Phentermine (capsule)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


Opened up a 37.5 mg phentermine capsule and snorted it in a series of 6 lines. The first 'bump' was like a very dull version of cocaine. It does not have the numbing properties of coke so it burns the nose a bit.

The phentermine made me feel awake and talkative and it totally suppresses my appetite. The good feeling lasted roughly 15 - 20 minutes. Then I took one line about every 15 minutes to keep it up. The feeling became more awake then high though. Not nearly as fun as coke, but I would do it again maybe before work as it really wakes me up.
