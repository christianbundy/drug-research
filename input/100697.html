0000 I'm out of my depth, my visual field is completely covered by repeated shapes. I look in my hand and droplets appear - a billion miracles forming.

My voice is strange - echoing.

I never expected this.

Dreadful nausea - I'm frightened.

0100 I float between feeling scared and joy. The nausea is passing. I lie on my bed and play my music.

0200 My dark room fills now with gargoyles and the walls breathe.

0230 Finally I find myself relaxing. The light that comes from behind begins to move forward and the gargoyles disappear.

I seek out the light and as I look I see an intense light as people describe in Near Death Experiences. But it is first behind perspex. My path is clear.

0300 Finally, fully joyful, the light - my communion with the divine - is fully visible and showers of beauty fall from it. I am an an 'atrium'. My audience with the divine light which lifts my spirits. All that is beautiful is a manifestation of the divine. And I am well stocked.

Large polls hold this immense place - but the light is independent. Perhaps this drug is the way we may come to the divine.

0400 Afterglow and much to say. Had I been cautious I would never have found such.
