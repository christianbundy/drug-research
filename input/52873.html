Pumping Myself Up
Modafinil (Provigil)
by Snowflake
Citation:   Snowflake. "Pumping Myself Up: experience with Modafinil (Provigil) (ID 52873)". Erowid.org. May 5, 2006. erowid.org/exp/52873

 
DOSE: 400 mg oral Modafinil (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


These things are great except I experienced severe anxiety. I have anxiety and I take medication for it, so its under control.

I havent had a problem with anxiety for years now.

I only took 2 Modafinils, which were 200 mg each. They worked excellent at not letting me feel tired the whole entire day, but the anxiety was so horrid that it actually wasnt worth it. I was so worried about ridiculous things and afraid I was getting fired for NO reason. I know u must be thinking that anxiety isnt THAT bad, but it was mind blowing bad! I felt so scared the whole day.

By the way, the pills are so horrible tasting.
