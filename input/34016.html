10+minutes - I feel a bit stoned, my fingers have slight, jittery trails as I am typing.

15+minutes - monitors refresh rate more noticable, appreciable connection to geometric/psychedelic patterns, pupils constricted

p.s I am going to try some more, intruiging to say the least.
