Forgotten Flight
Zolpidem (Ambien)
by Rory47
Citation:   Rory47. "Forgotten Flight: experience with Zolpidem (Ambien) (ID 88232)". Erowid.org. Nov 13, 2010. erowid.org/exp/88232

 
DOSE: 12.5 mg oral Pharms - Zolpidem (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


A few weeks ago I was flying from NY to Detroit. My flight kept getting delayed, so I decided to take my normal prescribed dose at 5:00pm instead of 11:00pm.

As it turned out, like 30 minutes later they started boarding and it was a only 2 hour flight. Though I do not remember it, I must have went unconscious on the airline.

I had airline blankets and 'Skymall' magazines when I woke up at my friends house the next day.

I had so little memory of that night, I needed to be reminded of just about everything. It was kind of scary, not knowing what happened. I was with a trustworthy friend, but if I was not, I would have a full night blank.
