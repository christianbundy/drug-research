Brainfog Lifted
Piracetam & Choline
by Krusey
Citation:   Krusey. "Brainfog Lifted: experience with Piracetam & Choline (ID 41466)". Erowid.org. Nov 1, 2007. erowid.org/exp/41466

 
DOSE: 3200 mg oral Piracetam (capsule)
  250 mg oral Vitamins / Supplements  

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


After 10 days of waiting, the mail order piracetam finally came. I downed four 800mg capsules with a 250mg choline chaser right before leaving for school, in hopes of improving my finals. The literature I read said not to expect much feeling from the piracetam, for it isn't meant to 'fuck you up.' That was a very true statement, because I didn't necessarily feel too different....just......better.

I felt like I had just been sick for a few days (even though I wasn't) and the sickness left. It was the 'I feel like a million bucks' type of feeling. The fog in my brain had lifted. I even wanted to talk to people for the first time in a while.
