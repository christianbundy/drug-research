Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine)

Citation:   anonymous. "Load of Shit!: experience with Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine) (ID 13274)". Erowid.org. Feb 23, 2005. erowid.org/exp/13274

 
DOSE: 500 mg oral Dimenhydrinate (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb


Well all I can say is...ITS SHIT!! I must say I had a few closed eye visuals, but other than that it felt like I had the flu.

I bought my pack of dramamine on Thursday, & I said I'd take it on Friday night. So on Friday at around 11:15pm I took my 10 pills (it only comes in packs of 10 where I live) chewing the first 5 by accident (which left my tongue all numb & a really bad taste) & swallowing the other 5. I waited for about 30 mins, but I couldn't stay awake so I went to sleep. I woke up around 1:00am (I think so, I didn't check what time it was) my head was spinning, but I wasn't having any hallucinations. I went back to sleep for about 10 mins, then woke up. I kept going to sleep for a few minutes then waking up, this carried on till around 6:00am. I finally went to sleep for about 2 hours. I then woke up at 8:00am, & I had to take a piss. When I got up out of bed & stood up, my head felt really light. When I tried walking my legs felt like they were made of concrete. I went to the toilet then went back to bed. I got up at 11:00am and went downstairs had a cup of coffee & watched wrestling, while I was watching wrestling closed my eyes & I could see stuff like skulls & shit. I thought that was pretty cool. It all lasted till around 3:00pm, then I felt normal again.

All in all it was OK, but I probably won't try it again. also my dick kinda shrinks and that totally scared me.
