Helps Alcohol Addiction
Trazodone & Alcohol
by Just One Guy
Citation:   Just One Guy. "Helps Alcohol Addiction: experience with Trazodone & Alcohol (ID 71241)". Erowid.org. May 18, 2008. erowid.org/exp/71241

 
DOSE: 150 mg oral Pharms - Trazodone (daily)
  1500 ml oral Alcohol - Beer/Wine (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


My experience with trazodone is very positive.

It helps with my alcohol addiction in that I can consume much less alcohol per day. Before the trazodone, I would need to consume at least 3 liters of, say Chardonnay, at 13% potency, per day to fall asleep. With 150mg trazodone, I need less than 1.5 liters
to fall asleep. I know that is still a lot of wine, but it helps me not to need so much wine.
