Fluffy Happy Feeling :)
Hydrocodone with Acetaminophen (Vicodin)
by WinterPrincess
Citation:   WinterPrincess. "Fluffy Happy Feeling :): experience with Hydrocodone with Acetaminophen (Vicodin) (ID 47132)". Erowid.org. Jun 25, 2009. erowid.org/exp/47132

 
DOSE: 7.5 mg oral Hydrocodone (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


One Saturday evening, I had the worst toothache of my life from a wisdom tooth that needed to be taken out, and my appt wasn't for 4 more days and I literally thought I was going to die from the horrific pain! I could hardly move. So I decided to go the emergency room to get something for the pain. The ER Doctor gave me Vicodin ES and Antibiotics. I got the scrips filled at 6pm and was home by 6:15pm and popped a Vicodin by 6:25pm. I had never taken Vicodin before, so I had NO idea what to expect.

6:35pm: I layed on my couch praying to God that this pill would take away the unforgiving pain that had me paralyzed. I hadn't eaten much but broth that day because my mouth hurt so badly.

6:55: I start to notice that not only is the pain lifting away, but when I rubbed my legs together it felt really good. So I started rubbing my hands together and feeling my arms, and had the same feeling. My state of mind was also changing. My spirit was lifting, and I had been in severe pain and very sad for days, so it was wonderful!]

7:10pm: I had started feeling pretty euphoric, and could move around and wanted to turn on the TV and ended up seeing that 'Shanghai Noon' had just started and I had never seen it before. So I started watching it.

7:30: The pain was mostly gone, I was laying on my couch, feeling light and fluffy! I kept wanting to rub my legs together and touch my hands, as it felt almost like a magical numbing feeling, that even though it was sort of numbing, I could still feel it and it was amazing!

8pm: I was still feeling wonderful, and laughing so hard at 'Shanghai Noon' like it was one of the funniest movies in the world! I had a sense of very high spirits, both mentally and physically, and emotionally. I was smiling and felt very content!

9pm: I still felt incredibly pleasent and the body high was one of the best I have ever experienced before.

After about 10pm I was getting pretty sleepy, probably due to not sleeping well all weekend from being in pain. I still felt good, but was coming down slowly and then passed out and slept like a baby.
