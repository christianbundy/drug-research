No Go
Hyoscyamine & Quentiapine (Seroquel)
by Narr
Citation:   Narr. "No Go: experience with Hyoscyamine & Quentiapine (Seroquel) (ID 72032)". Erowid.org. Dec 22, 2008. erowid.org/exp/72032

 
DOSE: 9.375 mg oral Hyoscyamine (pill / tablet)
  300 mg oral Pharms - Quetiapine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


I was trying to get the hallucinogenic effect of hyoscyamine on this trip, I took 25 tabs of .375mg. After 20 hours I figured nothing was happening then I realized that I was on a powerful anti-psychotic. It appears seroquel will arrest any hallucinogenic episodes, schizophrenic or drug induced.
