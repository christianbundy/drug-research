Did Absolutely Nothing
Catnip
by Terrance
Citation:   Terrance. "Did Absolutely Nothing: experience with Catnip (ID 94696)". Erowid.org. Feb 8, 2012. erowid.org/exp/94696

 
DOSE: 5 g smoked Catnip  
    oral Catnip (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 165 lb


I bought 25 grams of fresh, organic catnip. Tried it as a tea, and noticed only very mild relaxation. I then took many bong hits, followed by a massive joint, and felt absolutely nothing apart from a VERY mild mellow feeling. I've tried it again on numerous occasions, all to no avail. I understand that the effects of catnip vary from individual to individual, but for me it did next to nothing.
