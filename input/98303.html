Possibly Just Too Intense
Smoking Blends - Cannabis-Like
by Jayce
Citation:   Jayce. "Possibly Just Too Intense: experience with Smoking Blends - Cannabis-Like (ID 98303)". Erowid.org. Feb 26, 2013. erowid.org/exp/98303

 
DOSE: 0.1 g smoked Products - Smoking Blends - Cannabis-Like (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 285 lb


I quit smoking cannabis quite a while back, a professional precaution. When I did, though, I like most started with commercial brick, and worked up to 'kind' or the best local homegrown. Lately, I've been experimenting with herbal potpourri. I was fortunate enough to try the ol' trainwreck by haze. Awsome 018 blend. Last year I tried quite a few different blends. As I travel for work, I get the chance to try alot of different brands and mixes. I purchased a 3g package of Gorilla Dro about a month ago. Figuring it to be nothing more than a 'weak' substitute, I made the mistake of taking a small sample puff on the drive home. Fortunately I was only 3 blocks from my motel room.

[Erowid Note: Driving while intoxicated or tripping is dangerous and irresponsible because it endangers other people. Don't do it!]

I inhaled a small amount through a brand new unit, and then, like a ton of bricks it kicked in. I couldn't feel my feet. Hard to drive when you can't find your feet. Panic onset like a real 'physical' heart attack. It seemed to take a half hour to drive those three blocks. When I somehow managed to park my pickup, I was stuck. I couldn't put the vehicle in park, and my foot on the brake was all that was keeping the vehicle from moving. I sat in my truck for what must have been 20 minutes or more, almost in a commatose state with my mind wandering different planes of existance.

Suddenly I began to realize that I was regaining my basic motor skills. I put the vehicle in park, and stumbled to my room. For the next 5-7 uses, the effects would be very similar. An extremely powerful high, almost able to disconnect the brain from the physical body. Keep in mind these effects are being reported from a completely clean individual. I do drink every day, but nothing illegal goes into my body. I hadn't had any other potpourris's for quite a while... perhaps 4-5 mos.

I can't imagine being an inexperienced smoker and being introduced to this product inproperly. I have shared with others, but always let them know what they may experience, and I stick around to help guide them if they request help. Not for the casual user, certainly not for anyone under 21. Now that I've smoked around 6 grams over a couple months, the effects are much milder. The tolerance took me about a month to develop, and the trippy times were intense up until then.
