My Favorite Drug
Kratom (Premium Bali Powder)
by Poop
Citation:   Poop. "My Favorite Drug: experience with Kratom (Premium Bali Powder) (ID 81217)". Erowid.org. Feb 18, 2011. erowid.org/exp/81217

 
DOSE: 8 g oral Kratom (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 155 lb


I have used Kratom a couple of times and loved it each time. I have experimented with weed, percocet, xanax and alcohol and I have to say that Kratom is my favorite drug by far.

I usually use around 8 grams of powder. I take dissolve it in warm water than pour orange juice over it to diffuse the taste which I find works rather well.

In all my experiences I have found Kratom to induce a very relaxing and calm feeling. Also, I experience pretty intense euphoria for about 3 hours. After the 3 hour mark the effects start to fade and usually take another 3 hours to completely wear off.

One thing that does suck about Kratom is I am usually left with a headache and a burnt out feeling similar to weed. All in all, Kratom is awesome!
