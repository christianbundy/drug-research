Overly Romanticized
Cocaine
by Anonymous
Citation:   Anonymous. "Overly Romanticized: experience with Cocaine (ID 21677)". Erowid.org. Feb 23, 2006. erowid.org/exp/21677

 
DOSE: 3 lines insufflated Cocaine (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


Cocaine had always interested me for the fact that it is always the attention of anything drug related (government, movies, statues, etc). When I had the chance to try some, I decided that it wouldn't hurt if I tried a small amount.

My friend made up small line and I snorted it. Effects were nominal, if at all. Two small lines later, I still had discernable effect. I placed a bit on my gums and the numbing effect was fun, like going to the dentist and getting novocaine.

Ultimately, cocaine is overly romanticized, and my friends who also tried it agree. People make such a big deal about cocaine, but I do not anything remarkable about it. However, I can completely see the addiction potential. The cocaine left such a craving, it made me want to do line after line (which I did not do - 3 lines total).

I noticed no unpleasant effects throughout, but when some cannabis was smoked, the high was intense (perhaps due to quality green). All told, cocaine is interesting, but not worth the money or addiction potential.
