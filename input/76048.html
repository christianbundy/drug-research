Zolpidem (Ambien)

DOSE: 20 mg oral Pharms - Zolpidem (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 190 lb


I have a very severe case of insomnia. (2) Ambien 10mg have a very negligible effect on my getting to sleep. There may be a very fleeting feeling that sleep should occur, but it quickly wears off. I wouldn't call it 'sedation,' just a very mild drowsy feeling. In fact, because Ambien's effects are so short-lived, I can drive and do all normal functioning without any impairment.
