Helps with Withdrawal
Pregabalin (Lyrica) & Alprazolam (Xanax)
by Greg!
Citation:   Greg!. "Helps with Withdrawal: experience with Pregabalin (Lyrica) & Alprazolam (Xanax) (ID 83033)". Erowid.org. Dec 30, 2009. erowid.org/exp/83033

 
DOSE:   oral Pharms - Alprazolam (daily)
  300 mg oral Pharms - Pregabalin (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


I have been taking alprazolam (Xanax) for about 3 years now. I take 2mg three times a day, and now that dose just keeps me from going into withdrawals. I don't get much of a benzo high from 2mg anymore. I've been trying to ween off of it entirely, but lowering doses just don't seem to work. I can't sleep if I don't take it regularly.

I recently was prescribed pregabalin (Lyrica) for nerve pain, 150mg twice a day. The first day I took it, I remember feeling giddy and talkative and high spirited. I did some research and saw that it was an anticonvulsant that works on GABA similarly to benzodiazapines. With this I started lowering my alprazolam dose. Now I can just take 2mg a day instead of 6mg and I won't feel the withdrawals. It's only been 2 weeks and I went down 4mg in alprazolam easily, which would have taken me two months according to most tapering charts. Now I only hope I'm not switching one withdrawal to another.
