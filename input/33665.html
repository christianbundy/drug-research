Such a Head Change
Mushrooms
by Mug
Citation:   Mug. "Such a Head Change: experience with Mushrooms (ID 33665)". Erowid.org. Aug 19, 2006. erowid.org/exp/33665

 
DOSE:   smoked Mushrooms (dried)
    insufflated Mushrooms (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 130 lb


This has happened on two different occasions.

The smoking was first, heard rumors that it would fuck me up and hit me fast and wasnt like shrooming.

Well, I hit it, tasted horrible, smelt horrible and I considered it a waste of the cap immediately.

I got really giggle and a fast head change, even though my mouth tasted like burnt ass and fungus. Everything became quite woozy, and I was slightly dumb, like when I eat shrooms, but less oblivious to everything. A constant 'dur' moment.

Snorting was different.

I ground it up in a coffee grinder (which I also have ground up weed in). Me and my friend - who is 6'1 and weighs a lot more than me - did a 2 inch line each, he just said it hurt and didnt do much but make him laugh a little.

For me, (Ive never snorted anything else either), I got really giggle and happy FAST. I was like a little kid, yet still aware of everything. Just happy. Sound was more exquisite and I saw SLIGHT visuals (cottage cheese ceiling flowing and wall textures slowly moving).

It was pretty cool for such a small amount.
