Noticeable Shift at 4mg
2C-T-7
by Oldtimer
Citation:   Oldtimer. "Noticeable Shift at 4mg: experience with 2C-T-7 (ID 1702)". Erowid.org. Oct 10, 2000. erowid.org/exp/1702

 
DOSE: 4.0 mg oral 2C-T-7 (liquid)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


Carefully weighing out 120+-mg of pharmaceutical 2C-T-7, I added the crystals to 30 ml of dH2O resulting in a solution of approximately 4mg/ml.

4:00PM Off work, I take an 10cc oral dose syringe out (same one used to measure the water) and draw exactly 1cc (4 mg) of this solution and squirt unceremoniously into my mouth.

4:30 waiting

5:00 waiting

5:30 a slight alert, only barely noticeable

5:45 some mood elevation, slight nausea

6:00 now I am feeling the substance. It has a warm very rosy feel to it, even though it was preceded by uneasy stomach.

8:00 Ok, I know some will say it ain't so, but my curtains are revolving tubes of vertical colors. It isn't a huge visual, and it takes some staring, but it is there. This substance gives a secure and warm feeling. In a dose of 2mg it might be an excellent antidepressant. The edge of psychedelia is there even at 4mg, although I have heard it isn't active below 10mg.

10:00 Down completely, weird background echos, but otherwise normal.

excellent material. I recommend experimentation in both higher and lower dosages, perhaps as low as 1mg. Certainly I went over threshold at 4mg.
