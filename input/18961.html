Great Time
Tramadol (Ultram)

DOSE: T+ 0:00 150 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:20 50 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 135 lb


Well, being bored one night I remembered having about 15 Ultram(tramadol) in my possesion. How they got there is another story. Anyhow, after reading extensively into this topic, (as I do whenever I try any new drug.) I wanted to see what 150 mg would do. I had previously tried 50 and 100 Mg which did nothing. I later found out that they might have if I would've waited long enough before saying 'fuck it' and going to sleep.

So, I went upstairs and popped 3 of the 50mg pills. I glanced at the clock, it read 11:10. I was very excited to see what would happen. I retreated to the computer to read experience reports about other drugs and mess around on the internet. After about 20 minutes I decided I needed a stronger dose, so I popped another one. 2 hours had passed with no effects what so ever, and I totally gave up on Ultram as a recreational drug, thinking any of the effects described in experience reports we're purely a manifestation of the mind. Most likely the placebo effect. As I walked upstairs sad and defeated, the onset hit me. It felt incredably good. I was clearheaded and felt kind of out of myself. Pure Euphoria! I dimmed the lights and turned the TV on low. I felt like I was on top of the world. I would sometimes get lost in a train of thought and could be carried off to whatever I was thinking about. It was like a lucid dream when I closed my eyes.

I turned the TV off and just basked in the wonderfulness of this awesome drug. These effects stayed with me for a few hours, until I felt like I should get some sleep, around 3:30. At first it was difficult but it's always difficult for me to fall asleep. I was out in about 15 minutes. I had very pleasent dreams all through the night. I woke up feeling some slight nausea, and a little tired, but nothing like a hangover or anything. I still felt great, and I never threw up or anything. All in all I must say this experience was definately worth it! It was one of the greatest feelings ever. I would do it again, and probably will.
