Opiate Withdrawl and Anxiety Life Saver

Citation:   curious. "Opiate Withdrawl and Anxiety Life Saver: experience with Gabapentin (ID 98653)". Erowid.org. Jan 18, 2013. erowid.org/exp/98653

 
DOSE: 1800 mg oral Pharms - Gabapentin (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 235 lb


I was on percocet 10 for a year when my supply ran out. I went into withdrawal and my anxiety Came back. I was bed ridden and eating My klonopins with no relief.

I went to my moms House to see what she had and all she had was 4 bottles of samples of 100 mg Neurontin, 100 pills per bottle. She said it was a pain reliever so I took 6 pills and about an hour later I started getting a small buzz and my withdrawals were
gone. I felt normal and with no anxiety.

I am 4 days into this with no percocets and I still feel great. I'm taking 6 neurontin 3 times a day. No buzz after last day but I still feel great...it is a life saver.
