Cures One Problem, Brings On Another
Ginko biloba
by Rev.MRC
Citation:   Rev.MRC. "Cures One Problem, Brings On Another: experience with Ginko biloba (ID 19003)". Erowid.org. Jan 27, 2008. erowid.org/exp/19003

 
DOSE: 160 mg oral Ginkgo biloba (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 70 kg


After a metal concert I noticed a steady ringing noise in my ears that wasn´t supposed to go away, so the doc prescribed Infusions combined with ginko pills. The volume of those strange noise decreased to a less annoying level, cause of vasodilatation, I think. I felt slight enhancement in cerebral function, which is also combined with the effect of a subtle mood lift.

After taking ginko - pills for more than one year, TV and other screens caused nasty migraine-like headache. At first I thought this problem is linked to the noise the screens produce in interference with the noise in my head. I noticed furthermore that beer caused severe headache even in doses less than 0,5 l. Finally I noticed that I get
a headache if I don't take those ginko pills!!! Now I have to slowly reduce the dose to get rid of my headache problem. In addition I felt that I had to drink a very lot when taking ginko, which could also be linked to the headache.
