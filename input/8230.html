Mild Cerebral High
Mushrooms (smoked)
by jero
Citation:   jero. "Mild Cerebral High: experience with Mushrooms (smoked) (ID 8230)". Erowid.org. Jul 20, 2001. erowid.org/exp/8230

 
DOSE: 0.5 g smoked Mushrooms (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


Well,this being something i've experienced quite a few times ,the effects are generally the same.A mild cerebral high,similar to a toke or two off of some commercial grade bud.slightly more intense body sensations than the same...usually accompanied by some nice colored visuals....lasting 30 mins. to 1 hour....don't waste the caps or stems...use the shake and any dust in the bottom of the bag.
