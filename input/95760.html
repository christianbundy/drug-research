DOSE: .5 oz   Kratom (ground / crushed)


Take an oz of crushed and/or powdered kratom boil in a pot of water until half full, strain the plant matter, then set aside the water, replace the plant matter in the pot, and more water and boil again to half, strain plant matter again and throw away, combine the two batches of kratom water together and boil until it becomes a resin, that easy, typically yeilds around 15-20grams of kratom resin, roll in to balls the dose desired.
