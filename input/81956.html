Talkative and Alert
4-Fluoroamphetamine
by sia
Citation:   sia. "Talkative and Alert: experience with 4-Fluoroamphetamine (ID 81956)". Erowid.org. Oct 28, 2009. erowid.org/exp/81956

 
DOSE: 40 mg oral 4-Fluoroamphetamine (powder / crystals)
  1 mg oral Pharms - Lorazepam (pill / tablet)


I took 1mg of lorazepam and 40mg of 4-Fluoroamphetamine. The effects of the 4-FA were a little stronger than caffeine is just before it gets jittery.

I was talkative and alert, but without any jittering or jaw grinding. The effects passed after about 4 hours and no ill after effects were noted. I usually go for 80-120mg for full effects of this substance.

I took a drug test the next day for the military and 4-FA did not show up, but the lorazepam did.
