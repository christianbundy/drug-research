Psychedelic Candyland
2C-P & MDMA
by Xochipilli

DOSE: T+ 0:00 8 mg insufflated 2C-P (powder / crystals)
  T+ 0:30 2 mg oral 2C-P (powder / crystals)
  T+ 0:30 100 mg insufflated MDMA (powder / crystals)
  T+ 2:30 2 mg insufflated 2C-P (powder / crystals)
  T+ 2:30 40 mg insufflated MDMA (powder / crystals)
  T+ 4:20 60 mg insufflated MDMA (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


T+0: Initial dose, instant change of consciousness. Very excited for this.

T+1: walls beginning to breathe gently, feeling great! Looking forward to a heavy, colorful experience.

T+2: beginning to come up harder. Visual distortion is more pronounced. Auditory hallucinations beginning to manifest. General shift in consciousness has taken hold; this is a sarcastic-feeling drug so far; very in-your-face psychedelia. Beautiful so far, very smooth come-up, in waves only partially obscured by the brazen euphoria of mdma. I can tell this is going to be a very introspective, enlightening experience.

T+2:30 approx 2mg 2cp insufflated, approx 40mg mdma insufflated. Very pronounced rolling effects with steadily increasing psychedelia..

T+3: more and more of an edge to this trip; I'm starting to go nuts. The headspace created by this unique combo is nothing other than perfect. I feel very at peace with the universe, as well as the current situation I am in. Getting increasingly obvious OEVs and CEVs; distortion is becoming prominent. Heavier light trails The bodyload is hypnotic from 2cp and yet slightly hurried by the mdma. I can imagine this is what mescaline feels like; Ive heard the two compared, but its interesting to feel as though I could compare the two, even having not tried mescaline yet.. Facitious introspection is easy; this seems like a circuit junkies psychedelic wonderland. Im gonna have some fun tonight =p

T+4: more and more frequent waves of euphoria and relaxed stimulation. My stomach is a little off. I attribute this to the fact I ingested a phenethylamine, lol. Gonna get lost in the next two hours, cant wait!

T+4:20: 60mg mdma insufflated. Crazy high.

[at this point we go on a very fulfilling journey to the woods. I was lost in the world the entire time, beautiful experience..]

T+8: visuals are sparse, underwhelmed by the lack of intensity. Everyones pretty absorbed by their phones. Im thinking about T+12 and T+13

T+9: smoking most definitely obscures of the high. Right now life looks unrealthe morning sky through the window is a painting. Love this.

T+12:30: I slept through about 3 hours of my plateau, woke up feeling refreshed and still tripping, although I would venture to guess this is the start of the come-down. Time for some rocket fuellll ;)

T+15: Auditory distortion still pronounced, Some visual inaccuracies. Very beautifuld day out, I feel as though the pleateau hasnt worn off yet..about to insufflate 200mg MDMA and see how it goes
