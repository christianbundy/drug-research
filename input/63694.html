Odd Panic
Melatonin
by New Fall
Citation:   New Fall. "Odd Panic: experience with Melatonin (ID 63694)". Erowid.org. Jun 15, 2007. erowid.org/exp/63694

 
DOSE: 3.0 mg oral Melatonin (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 184 lb


I had just heard of Melatonin helping people get to sleep, so I went out to the grocery store and bought some.

T=10:45 - Took 3 mg Melatonin with a glass of water

T+0:15 - Very heavy tired feeling, craving sleep. I decided to get ready for bed and lie down.

T+0:20 - Heart rate feels like it is increasing, I am very tired but I cannot close my eyes.

T+0:30 - I feel like I am having a panic attack, I am not. Harder to breathe, difficult to form positive thoughts.

T+0:35 - Seems like I am heading back to baseline, except with a slight headache. Still a little difficult to breathe, and my left eye hurts.

T+0:37 - I try using my muscles but it seems like they are too weak to do anything. Still trying to fall asleep, but can't.

T+0:40 - Still trying to sleep.

That was the last entry in the journal, no vivid dreams, sleep came harder than usual, and it was a horrid time.
