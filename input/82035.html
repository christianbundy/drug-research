Disappointing
MDMA (Ecstasy) & Escitalopram (Lexapro)
by Collegestudent
Citation:   Collegestudent. "Disappointing: experience with MDMA (Ecstasy) & Escitalopram (Lexapro) (ID 82035)". Erowid.org. Nov 2, 2009. erowid.org/exp/82035

 
DOSE: 10 mg oral Pharms - Citalopram (daily)
  1 tablet oral MDMA (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 130 lb


I have been on Lexapro 10mg for about 2 months and took ecstasy this past weekend, just one roll. I did drink a little bit of alcohol, but a negligent amount, and stayed hydrated. I would just like to advise future Lexapro/SSRI users about the experience; I have taken ecstasy 4 or 5 times before while not on antidepressants and this time the roll was NOT WORTH IT. It was not necessarily a bad time, but it was disappointing because everyone with me was rolling hard while I was not really feeling it.
