When Looking Down, Life is Beautiful
Methylphenidate & Cannabis
by Von Toffle
Citation:   Von Toffle. "When Looking Down, Life is Beautiful: experience with Methylphenidate & Cannabis (ID 15118)". Erowid.org. Jun 24, 2007. erowid.org/exp/15118

 
DOSE: 10 mg insufflated Pharms - Methylphenidate (ground / crushed)
  0.3333 g smoked Cannabis (plant material)
  20 mg insufflated Pharms - Methylphenidate (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 180 lb


After finally getting a chance to satisfy my curiousity with methylphenidate (ritilin) I feel the need to document it.

Setting: Last nite, due to a lit paper, I only got 4 hours of sleep. After school I procured 5 generic ritilin tabs, and was already anxious as hell to go home and get high. Later, these were confirmed to be 10mg, methylphenidate after a google search on the markings. I also have not snorted anything and my previous drug experiences has been with just pot & hydrocodone, not even alcohol.

Starting roughly about 5-6 o'clock pm I crushed one of the tabs with the help of a glass bottle which was housing a delightful bamboo plant, and some school stationary. I had to break apart some pieces with a pin. I snorted the 10mg, and noticed little burn, shortly after I felt some very mild effects: more alert, heart beat, and was a little jittery. Then I smoked about 1/3 of a gram of pot followed by another 20mg snorted. after about 10mins I was plenty high, and enjoying it immensely. I was spacy yet filled with energy and couldnt stop bobbing my head to the music I was listening to very loudly.

I was also a little more coherent then I am with just pot. As to be expected thoughts just flowed marvelously. However, they weren't the usual melancholic nihilistic ones, they were more optimistic and I decided I enjoyed life immensely. I also noticed a little nasal drip, but it wasnt bad, neither was the light chemical taste in my mouth. after about an hour, I could feel both drugs, winding down, but the ritilin left more abruptly and the resonating pot felt great still. So it was a smooth transistion, I went from highly energic to feeling almost completely exhausted.

But again, it still felt great, I was tempted to snort more, but I resisted. 3-4 hours later I had a subtle nausea, which when sitting, bothered me little. Today, I wanted to get high again and snort more ritilin, but thats a common pitfall I have, I am far too indulgent. caving in at about 11 o'clock I snorted 20mg, and topped it off with a hot shower in the dark. I noticed little this time, just energy and a boost in confidence. This pretty much concludes my introductory experiences with ritilin, and I hope to have more. Overall, I tend to still like opiates and pot better, but stimulants like ritilin are delightful as well.
