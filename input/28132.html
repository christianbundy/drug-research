An Effective Dose Level
4-Acetoxy-MiPT & Cannabis
by Duggin
Citation:   Duggin. "An Effective Dose Level: experience with 4-Acetoxy-MiPT & Cannabis (ID 28132)". Erowid.org. May 19, 2006. erowid.org/exp/28132

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 8.0 mg oral 4-Acetoxy-MiPT (liquid)
  T+ 0:08   smoked Cannabis  
  T+ 0:50   smoked Cannabis  

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


Mindset & Setting: I was tired going into this. I'd been up for a day and a half, but got sleep the night before this trip. I didn't feel particularly tired, but I knew my body was.

Experience: This was my first time trying this substance.

Preparation: The 4-AcO-MiPT I had was very sticky, and in the time it took to weigh my dose out (just a few minutes), it had gotten twice as dark as its original tan color. It was now brown. It dissolved over a matter of minutes into my cup of water (without stirring), and was in 2 stuck-together chunks when I dropped it in. It's so sticky and clumpy that it would be nearly impossible to chop it up into powder.

------------------------------------------------------

9:48 start drinking my water with 8 mg 4-aco-mipt HCl in it. This stuff tastes horrible. I can taste it with every drink. It's pretty bitter.

9:56 start smoking a joint with my water.

10:05 done drinking the water. I only smoked a quarter of that joint. I'm high for sure.

10:13 I'm seeing trails from my arms moving, etc. and I'm VERY FUCKING HIGH. I am very cold as well.

10:15 I'm seeing awsome patterns in the floor. and on the walls.

10:17 A bit of tremoring, it's like parts of my body have shivers, and others don't.

10:20 I got under my covers on my bed and the shivering stopped. I was pretty cold. I just turned the fan off. Not so cold anymore.

Visuals are pretty strong now. Great patterning everywhere now, and I'm getting aural hallucinations of the computer fan wahing.

10:31 patterning when I close my eyes. heavy visuals from this stuff. strangely enough, visuals are heavy with the lights out as well.

10:38 I smoke more of the joint.

10:45 tripping pretty hard. not so hard I can't type though.

10:48 it looks like the wall is the floor and I'm penned up agains a wall when I lean back in my chair.

10:49 my perceptions are changing a lot, but I think I could still hold a conversation with someone. I'll see when my brother come in in a minute.

...can't really understand some of the stuff on TV. Communicating with my brother is relatively easy.

11:35 I'm stoned. colors are changing every now and then. I'm really tired from having stayed up the last few days... I don't think the joint helped my tired state. I just want to go to bed again.

11:41 bed.

12:24 so tired but I can't sleep. I lay on my bed, not wanting to get up, but I can't fall asleep. The weed is wearing off now. The visuals are still pretty much here. As well the aural hallucinations
12:39 I'm definitely coming down, and not tripping as hard as I was. I just smoked that J down to the paper and I still feel this way about the 4-aco-mipt. Visuals are still here, but it's just less intense now. the visuals just don't move as fast now.

...Going back to bed. I'm tired, but I just can't fall asleep so far. It's very strange.

1:45 hallucinations are still there, but they're not as intense or plentiful as they were. Not overtaking like they were. I can't sleep.

2:53 things are chilling out. I'm returning to 'baseline' but things are still giving minor trails/other patterning like visuals.

3:40 things aren't moving around that much. Patterns on the ceiling are there, but not moving fast at all and tracers are minor.

4:20 visuals almost all gone. slight patterning on the ceiling. slight tracers.

4:52 almost baseline. I'm hungry and tired. going to go to bed.

Sleep was good, had interesting dreams. I am amazed that this dose level was so effective. I'd like to take this substance again sometime when I'm not so tired.
