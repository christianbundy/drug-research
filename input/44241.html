Lighting the Sand
2C-D
by Dust Mote VII
Citation:   Dust Mote VII. "Lighting the Sand: experience with 2C-D (ID 44241)". Erowid.org. May 9, 2006. erowid.org/exp/44241

 
DOSE: 25 mg oral 2C-D (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 115 lb


My very first time was a subtle experience; I kept waiting for the ground to crack and to hallucinate things that weren't there.

I was at the beach in daylight and I was there with my newly ex boyfriend. While coming up I felt paranoid and insecure. I couldn't stop talking even though I felt babyish and annoying for doing it. I went for a walk to seperate myself and things got a little better.

Everything had a rainbow circumference to it, a pulsating gas-in-liquid effect but nothing too interesting. Seagulls were HUGE and very confusing. I found something which looked like a horse skull in the sand, but it must have really been a horseshoe crab. I was full of emotion and all the senses were enhanced: sand grains were large and grapefruit tasted so good I ate pieces which had sand on them.
