Fighting the Nod
Adrafinil
by AGW
Citation:   AGW. "Fighting the Nod: experience with Adrafinil (ID 46342)". Erowid.org. Jun 14, 2007. erowid.org/exp/46342

 
DOSE: 1 tablet oral Adrafinil

BODY WEIGHT: 120 kg


I found Adrafinil whilst researching stimulants. I do a lot of driving for my work and sometimes on a Friday evening I can nod off in the car due to extreme tiredness.

I got Adrafinil from a website and tried it the next time I noticed I was dropping off at the wheel. The amazing thing is that normally when I am that tired, very little, including winding the window down, playing loud music etc, works. Caffeine doesn't even work when I am that tired. 1 tablet of Adrafinil and 20 minutes later its as if all feelings of tiredness had completely gone. It was as if I had woken up from a long sleep fully refreshed.

I could get to sleep 3 or four hours later, although I did notice that my sleep is not as deep as normal. I also noticed that the tablet works for approximately 18-20 hours before wearing off.
