What the ?
Salvia divinorum
by That green jesus
Citation:   That green jesus. "What the ?: experience with Salvia divinorum (ID 23165)". Erowid.org. Mar 24, 2006. erowid.org/exp/23165

 
DOSE: 4 bowls smoked Salvia divinorum (leaves)
  1 bowl smoked Salvia divinorum (extract - 10x)

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


This was my first experence with salvia. I had recently ordered some salvia from an online dealer , they said it should take about a week to get to me. I am still waiting for my delivery and I'm pretty sure I won't be getting it. I really don't want to say any names but this place ripped me off. Not to worry I have already contacted the better business bureau.

I did not let this experience get me down, I was really wanting to try this stuff. So I ordered from another company, I got this shipment within 3 days (very pleased to say). Well as soon as I got it I wanted to try it after waiting so long. I wasn't quite sure if it was a good thing to do on this day because I was battling depression all day long but I said screw it I'll give it a shot. So I packed a couple of bowls of just the leaf and wasn't quite feeling anything, after the third bowl I started to feel a little funny. A little laughing was going on and I could see watery visuals around my room (nothing intense or anything) so I packed another bowl and still I pretty much felt the same except a little more dazed.

About 15 minutes later I thought maybe I'll try a little of the extract so I packed a little less than a whole bowl, I must of took about all of it in on one hit I really don't remember what happened next. As soon as I started to exhale my whole room just like flipped on its side and created a mirror image of itself completely surrounding me like every thing was shrinking. Every thing on my wall enlongated to make it seem like it was falling to the floor. I don't quite know what I was saying but it definitely felt like someone was in the room with me (I was alone when I did this). It felt like I was talking to a female and I was saying where am I whats going on and its not a bad thing is it. When I started coming to I realized how long I had been in this trance. Before I took that hit I had started my favorite cd (george acosta's awake) and I looked at the counter and it was almost half way over. To me it didn't feel like time existed.

I really didn't know what I was doing, so I will never do this again without a sitter because I don't even remember putting my pipe down or even exhaling.
