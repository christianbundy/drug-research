Experimenting with Doses
2C-E
by Happy Catipilar
Citation:   Happy Catipilar. "Experimenting with Doses: experience with 2C-E (ID 49148)". Erowid.org. Jun 21, 2007. erowid.org/exp/49148

 
DOSE:   oral 2C-E (capsule)
    insufflated 2C-E (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I found myself unemployed with free time between school schedules, so I thought I'd put it to good use.

[Erowid Note: A volume amount of one powder doesn't necessarily weigh the same as the same volume of another powder. For substances active as such a small dose, eyeballing is particularly unwise. See related article]

From known weights, usually 250 mg or 100 mg, I have eyeballed divisions to guess at my pill weights. So there is probably a +/-5% involved.

This report is specifically for a series of trials with 2C-E.

12.5 mg ingested orally proves very visual, but physiologically overwhelming, vomiting is probably a given.

8.66 mg orally relieves the physical, although the emotionally unstable my still have a bit of difficulty integrating (and will still pop.) This is my dose in snuff, a bit harder than the 12.5 mg oral dose, but 2c-e has an effect within 15 minutes by this route, otherwise it can take an hour to two hours.

2ct-2 trials, 31.25 mg oral.

The trip was amazing, the visuals were almost constant... distinctly distortions of what I was seeing though, nothing manifested as with a pot/acid combo. I make mention of this chem though, for it has greater potential as a synergist. 2c-e, usually at 10 mg with 20-25mg 2ct-2 creates oceans out of carpet, rainbows from white walls, etc.

Trips are usually visual for 5 hours after onset; for 2C-E and 2c-I. The visuals are pretty hard to ignore the first 3 of those (I refer to it as the self-guided phase).

2C-E becomes unbearable past 2x a dose.
