Vertiginous Mess
1,4-Butanediol
by GreenRaine
Citation:   GreenRaine. "Vertiginous Mess: experience with 1,4-Butanediol (ID 5333)". Erowid.org. Mar 1, 2001. erowid.org/exp/5333

 
DOSE: 3 caps oral 1,4-Butanediol (liquid)

BODY WEIGHT: 212 lb


I too tried Butanediol. While I enjoyed the nodding off sort of high it gave me (kind of reminded me of opiates), I didn't enjoy what happened when I finished my bottle. I had been hitting it daily until the bottle ran out. I was fine for a day, then I started feeling 'shaky' and developed vertigo (room spinning) so badly I had to hold onto the walls at work. I actually threw up from the vertigo. I also developed this nasty sweat on my feet, and my palms and hair also felt sweaty. I also felt that my neck was stiff and achy. This went on for a day or so, then passed. I won't be doing it again. Who knows what's in the industrial chemical version of the stuff which is being sold as printer cleaner, floor cleaner, etc...
