A Lift from Dysphoria
Moclobemide
by NN
Citation:   NN. "A Lift from Dysphoria: experience with Moclobemide (ID 92022)". Erowid.org. Jul 20, 2011. erowid.org/exp/92022

 
DOSE: 600 mg oral Moclobemide

BODY WEIGHT: 75 kg


After first week taking moclobemide (Aurorix), I notice quick improvement in mood, sleep, and interest of things.

Aurorix practically has no anticholinergic effect, no sedative effect, virtually non toxic to the liver.

As it is a RIMA, with the therapeutic dose, there is no need for food restriction.

I tried most antidepressants, but found this one works well for me.

It relieves my dysphoric feeling.
