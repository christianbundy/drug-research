No More Than a Placebo
Modafinil (Provigil)
by anonymous
Citation:   anonymous. "No More Than a Placebo: experience with Modafinil (Provigil) (ID 50614)". Erowid.org. May 1, 2006. erowid.org/exp/50614

 
DOSE: 600 mg oral Modafinil (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 235 lb


Provigil was prescribed to me for excessive sleepiness,I have obstructive sleep apnea. I tried 200 mg for 4 days then upped to 400 mg for 4 days then 600 mg for 4 days and didn't notice any effect whatsoever, not even a side effect. The script with insurance for 30 pills still cost $50.00.

My experience is that it is no more than a placebo.
