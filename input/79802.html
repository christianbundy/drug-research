DOSE: T+ 0:00 5 mg oral Pharms - Olanzapine (daily)
  T+ 0:00 100 mg oral MDMA  
  T+ 2:00 .5 g smoked Mushrooms (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 270 lb


I have a manic disorder. I also really like to trip. Now that I am on Zyprexa this is interestingly difficult most of the time. I recently attempted to enjoy MDMA. I've done it pre-Zyprexa and liked it a lot. I took the MDMA orally (I'm always on the Zyprexa, 5mg per day). I got some effect, mostly just heightened physical sensations, and an overall feeling of well-being. Unfortunately, about 2 hours later I also had the beginnings of a nasty migraine. To resolve the headache, I smoked about half a gram of primordia (baby psylocibe mushrooms. 10 minutes later the headache was gone. I never did get much more out of the MDMA, but hey - a feeling of well-being isn't a bad thing.
