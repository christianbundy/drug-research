Not Quite Enough
Codeine
by Echele
Citation:   Echele. "Not Quite Enough: experience with Codeine (ID 3033)". Erowid.org. Oct 13, 2000. erowid.org/exp/3033

 
DOSE: 120 mg oral Codeine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


Well, I had a bottle of Codeine in my medicine cabinet and I decided to get high off of it. I wasn't quite sure how much to take to get the full effect from it so I took 4 pills (30 mg each). After an hour I felt a slight buzz I guess you could say, so I had a couple of beers thinking that would intensify the feeling, but no go.

It wasn't bad. The problem I had was that I probably didn't consume enough codeine to really feel it. All I got was a slight buzz for about 2 hours and my coordination was off a bit also. I also ate before I took it because I heard of people vommiting after taking codeine. I had no upset stomach at all though. That's it.
