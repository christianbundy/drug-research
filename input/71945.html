I Quit Smoking
Hydrocodone & Tobacco
by Mark
Citation:   Mark. "I Quit Smoking: experience with Hydrocodone & Tobacco (ID 71945)". Erowid.org. Jun 18, 2008. erowid.org/exp/71945

 
DOSE: 5.0 mg oral Hydrocodone (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 185 lb


I was prescribed hydrocodone after some routine oral surgery. I had been trying to quit smoking off and on for years. The chemical depression of stopping always got the best of me and within a few days, I would give up my attempt.

Quitting with hydrocodone is a breeze. No depression, irritability, or cravings. There was the thought of smoking, but it had no urgency. I took the hydrocodone as directed, and when I ran out, that was it.

I no longer smoke and had no problem with needing any more opiates - although it the opportunity arises ....
