Hell
Caffeine
by Dumb Kid
Citation:   Dumb Kid. "Hell: experience with Caffeine (ID 35740)". Erowid.org. Jan 26, 2010. erowid.org/exp/35740

 
DOSE: 700 mg oral Caffeine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 45 kg


One day I was bored, so I decided to check our medicine cabinet to see what drugs we have. I found a bottle of caffeine pills, so I downed 7 pills. I had read up on it, not expecting anything special, just some energy.

Bit of energy.. right. I spent the next 9 hours in my bed shaking and feeling sick. It felt like constant panic attacks. I finally fell asleep and felt tired the next morning.

And yes, I'm skinny, so 700 mg affected me. To think that I almost took 15 pills (about 15 cups of coffee). Caffeine's good and all, just don't take too much.
