Insomnia Help
Melatonin
by King Fenix
Citation:   King Fenix. "Insomnia Help: experience with Melatonin (ID 63696)". Erowid.org. Jun 12, 2007. erowid.org/exp/63696

 
DOSE: 12 mg oral Melatonin (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


I have been taking Melatonin for about 6 months now and I started taking it because I would lie in bed for about an hour to an hour and a half. My doctor recommended it to me and he first recommended I take 9 mg 20 minutes before I go to bed, but after a while I started taking 6 mg then that wasn't working, so I decided to take 12 and then just recently saw that same doctor and he said that 12 was perfect for me right now and I have never slept better in my life.
