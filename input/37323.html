The Groovy Glide
Tramadol (Ultracet) & Cannabis
by Merry & Winter
Citation:   Merry & Winter. "The Groovy Glide: experience with Tramadol (Ultracet) & Cannabis (ID 37323)". Erowid.org. Jun 25, 2007. erowid.org/exp/37323

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 75 mg oral Pharms - Tramadol (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:00 650 mg oral Acetaminophen (pill / tablet)
  T+ 0:15 0.5 g smoked Cannabis (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


Today I acquired a few ultracet pills. I did a little research on the net and decided on an dose of two pills. This yields a total dose of 75 mg tramadol/650 mg acetaminophen. The cannabis I had was a very high grade hydro. I'm unsure of the exact strain, but it is very good. Like you haven't heard that before.

T-1:30
I ate a full dinner.

T+0:00
I took both ultracets orally.

T+0:15
Smoked approx. .5 grams of high grade bud.

T+45 mins
I think I start to feel something other than the weed. A little bit opiate in nature but definitely something else to it. I feel quite alert.

T+1 hr 15 min
Not quite sure I've hit the plateau because it keeps getting hard to type. Excellent euphoric body sensations. The torso feels so light.

T+1 hr 40 min
Good friend called conversation was fluid and warm. Conversations with this particular friend usually go this way but it was better this time.

T+2 hr 25 min
Past peak still going strong more weed or another ultracet will spike me back up.

Weed definately amplifies the action of this drug. I did not feel any of the nausea that others have reported with this drug. I also feel 'speedy' as others have reported but I have noticed this with opiates antagonist drugs ( oxycoden, hydracoden, codine, etc. ). B+ combo. A little short lived for my taste, but to be expected considering the ingredients.
