The Students Drug
Methylone
by DreamManTom
Citation:   DreamManTom. "The Students Drug: experience with Methylone (ID 97747)". Erowid.org. Oct 1, 2012. erowid.org/exp/97747

 
DOSE: 147 mg oral Methylone

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


I have tried methylone twice before (103 oral, 110 intranasal) before trying 147 oral, and I enjoyed it thoroughly.

0:00 I orally ingested 147mg methylone mixed in water with KoolAid.

0:10 Come up effects begin with out fail. In other experiences I noticed the timeliness of this drug is impressive. I feel light and bouncy, and as if I have a reserve of energy that I can choose to expend or not depending on my situation. I feel sober even though it is obvious that I am on a substance.

0:30 I am interested in any topic that is presented, and I continuously ask questions because of this. Conversation is easy and natural, and I experience joy by simply listening to my friend talk about something he is interested in. Focusing is not difficult, and complex thinking can be performed same as in sobriety.

0:50 I feel a sudden drop in my energy like the feeling of a stopping car. I am still enjoying conversation, but I do not ask questions or talk as quickly as before. At this point I am enjoying talking and chilling, while before I wanted to talk and know as much as possible. My drive to talk on this drug is out of interest, not out of stimulation.

1:10 I work on homework as I continue to converse with my friends. It is easy and enjoyable to do homework (genetics) which is surprising to me, and I do not get too distracted from my friends. I feel comfortable talking about any topic, and open to discuss personal ideas and problems.

There were light after effects the next day, consisting of having a good day.

While on this drug I termed it to myself as the 'Good Day Drug' because its effect is a concentration of how I feel when I am having a very good day.
