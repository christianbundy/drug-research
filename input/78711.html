Divine Incense
Salvia divinorum
by Wise_Sage
Citation:   Wise_Sage. "Divine Incense: experience with Salvia divinorum (ID 78711)". Erowid.org. May 12, 2009. erowid.org/exp/78711

 
DOSE:   inhaled Salvia divinorum (leaves)


I use Salvia as an incense as it is intended for. First I purify some dried leaves with copal resin placed on a hot coal. I like the smell of copal, and it is good aid for meditation. After some time I then take the leaves and crush them and drop them on the coal. Then I just enjoy the fragrance of the leaves as they burn on the coal with the copal.

The incense gives me a very relaxing and anti-depressant, 'glowing' feeling, a lucid clarity of mind and a sense of well being. If done before sleep I will have incredible dreams and dream recall, and many times they will be lucid dreams. If used as intended, it is a beneficial for aid for meditation.
