I Had Been in a Ghost Town
DPT
by DPT
Citation:   DPT. "I Had Been in a Ghost Town: experience with DPT (ID 31668)". Erowid.org. Jul 17, 2013. erowid.org/exp/31668

 
DOSE: 150 mg insufflated DPT (powder / crystals)

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


It all started when my friend R took some DPT at about 5 in the evening. He had expirienced a very bad trip, throwing up, unhappiness, the whole nine yards. Then at about 9 in the evening I had the insane urge to trip. I have tripped on DPT before and I was prepared for a very intense few hours.
I had him pick me up and then we went to the local store to buy some drinks. I insuffiliated about 150mg of DPT, and then pounded a drink to kill the god awful flavor. As we started to drive around I started to feel a little tingley on the inside. It took about 10 minutes for it to completely kick in. Then before I knew it some girl ended up in his car. I had already said to him 'I will see you in a few hours'. Things started to swirl a little and I had felt in a place of emptiness. I had felt like I had been in a ghost town and I was very unaware of where I was.

As we started to drive my visions became much more intense and I was becoming even more amazed by the vibrant images that where going through my mind. We had put a Sublime CD into the player and then the trip started really turning around. Everything became more like a dream worl that nothing could remove me from. I started to sweat a lot and then a minute later had become very cold the next second. I was very unaware of what was going on by this point. Tunnel vission cant even describe the visions I was seeing through my eyes. Everything was very pleasant and nothing could make me upset.

As I continued deeper into my trip I couldnt do anything for more than a few seconds. I was constantly opening and closing the window. Also I was amazed by the images that the cd deck where showing, vibrant colors were all I could see through my eyes.

I hacve tripped inside a house and it is one set place that cannot change and the environment will not change. While I was being driven around by a 'sober' driver, every different place we went to would bring my trip to another level. For instance: If we were going through a woodsy road and there was snow on the ground I wouuld feel very cold and lost, but then I would heat up very fast. Now when we were in a very lit place everything was looking like color sketches of the world. Everything around me was being drawn in as I went further and further into my trip.

We drove on and on and I put in a Led Zepplin cd in. Now let me tell you that old music really gets me going when I am tripping. It just gets me off and I love it. Makes me feel that much more comfortable. But anyways we drove around and I would look outside every now and then and it was a very blury immage. My friends told me that the entire trip I was just saying 'I HATE THIS!!!' and for some reason I can understand that completely. The trip came to an abrupt stop once we pulled into a McDonalds and it felt like I had just awoken out of a good night sleep or something. I was amzed by this DPT trip. As I would do this again in a hart beet.
