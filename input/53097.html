Rising and Drifting into Sleep
Zopiclone
by Velm
Citation:   Velm. "Rising and Drifting into Sleep: experience with Zopiclone (ID 53097)". Erowid.org. Jun 8, 2006. erowid.org/exp/53097

 
DOSE: 15 mg oral Pharms - Zopiclone (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 127 lb


After about 30 minutes my body becomes heavy. This is further highlighted amusingly when I attempt to walk and discoVer that my legs feel as though weights have been tied to them and as a result, I have to be careful not to topple over, side-ways into walls or worse.

My mind becomes indifferent, relaxed and I am left feeling this almost amorous craving for my bed clothes and pillow. Off I trot with a confuddled smile.

Once in bed, it won't take me long to curl up and fall into a pleasant, deeply refreshing sleep.

I found my state during the day to be lofty, radiant and relaxed. For a short-term aid I've now been taking doses daily for months to alleviate severe sleep problems.
