The Pleasing Smoke
Psychotria viridis, Nicotiana rustica & Cannabis
by N. Black
Citation:   N. Black. "The Pleasing Smoke: experience with Psychotria viridis, Nicotiana rustica & Cannabis (ID 51017)". Erowid.org. Feb 22, 2010. erowid.org/exp/51017

 
DOSE: 1/3 bowl smoked Psychotria viridis (dried)
  1/3 bowl smoked Tobacco (dried)
  1/3 bowl smoked Cannabis (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 260 lb


This is an amazingly calming smoke with the ability to take the negative feelings of a situation and turn them around completly with the first hit. Plus, the nicotiana rustica provides an awsome boost of energy which gets rid of those lethargic feeling sometimes associated with weed. On the other hand the psychotria viridis and the weed alone make for a great sleep aid to be used with calea zacatechichi (dream herb).
