The Starting Dose
Olanzapine
by Manic
Citation:   Manic. "The Starting Dose: experience with Olanzapine (ID 52762)". Erowid.org. Apr 30, 2006. erowid.org/exp/52762

 
DOSE:   oral Pharms - Olanzapine (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 13.5 kg


I started on 5mg per night to help me sleep. It had no noticable effect.

They upped the dose to 7.5mg, the only effect I felt was a slight strange feeling in my eyes.

I'm yet to try 10mg but I was told that psychiratrists sometimes start people on 10mg who have poor mental health. My mental health is mild, I suffer from mild schizophernia.
