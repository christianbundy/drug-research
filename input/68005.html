Beautiful and Fulfilling
Cocaine & Oxycodone
by Just for Fun
Citation:   Just for Fun. "Beautiful and Fulfilling: experience with Cocaine & Oxycodone (ID 68005)". Erowid.org. Jan 13, 2008. erowid.org/exp/68005

 
DOSE: 1.0 g insufflated Cocaine (powder / crystals)
  30 mg insufflated Pharms - Oxycodone (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


I am an occasional cocaine user (once a month roughly) and an occasional oxycontin user. I am not too fond of the 'crash' that comes from cocaine use, so I thought maybe using oxycontin would help assist in the 'coming down'. Over the past several hours I have snorted about a gram of cocaine. I then crushed up and snorted an oxycontin pill which was 30mg about fifteen minutes ago.

I am surprised about how much better I feel than just cocaine alone. My heart doesn't seem to be beating rapidly, and I feel very calm and relaxed. I can still feel the effects from the cocaine, as I stopped at the same time as starting the oxycontin. The 'crash' is not really a crash at all, but more like a whole new high that is beautiful and fulfilling. I suppose I am a little concerned about what kind of effects that could have on my body negatively, but overall I am really enjoying myself.
