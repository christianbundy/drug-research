Well, the next day, I looked it up on the internet, and the last war they were in was World War 2!

So because I was in such a great mindset that night, I've came up eith a proposition that I will live by and crusade for the rest of my life. This is my proposition and belief: LEGALIZATION=WORLD PEACE. Everyone I have talked to has agreed to this statement.
