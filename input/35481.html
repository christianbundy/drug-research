DOSE: 40 mg oral Pharms - Atomoxetine (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 165 lb


Strattera is a relatively new (as of May 2004) ADD medication. It feels much like an amphetamine without the jaw clenching, restlessness, or dry throat. One 40mg pill jacks me up pretty good for 24 hours, but I never seem to have trouble getting to sleep on it. It takes upwards of a week of daily use to build up a noticeable tolerance. Best of all, it doesnt show up on a drug test; I know this for a fact because my dad gave me a drug test while I was on it and it turned up negative. I can drink while on it, but steer clear of amphetamines, ephedrine, or anything else that could increase my heart rate or blood pressure any further. Dont take more than 100mg in a day. I notice the effects most when I sit down and do something engaging, like watch TV or read.
