Possible Immunity?
Mushrooms - P. Cubensis
by Matt
Citation:   Matt. "Possible Immunity?: experience with Mushrooms - P. Cubensis (ID 72514)". Erowid.org. Sep 16, 2008. erowid.org/exp/72514

 
DOSE:   repeated oral Mushrooms - P. cubensis (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 190 lb


About 2 years ago, I purchased 1/4 oz. of Cubensis. I was taking 10 mg (I believe) of Paxil a day. In previous years, I had taken Wellbutrin, Zoloft, Tegretol, Rittilan, Lithium, Dexadrin (sp?) and a couple of others that I can't recall the name of. I ate approx. 1/8 oz. of the Cubensis on a nearly empty stomach and toked a bowl of marijuana. I noticed the usual giggly high that I was getting from the marijuana, but after 2 hours, I decided that I was not feeling any effects, and proceeded to eat the other 1/8 oz. I stayed up for another 4 hours after that, and felt nothing more than how I would feel if I had been smoking marijuana only. Also, I would like to note that I purchased the mushrooms from a reliable source, and he tripped pretty heavy off of the same stash.

This last weekend, I went to the Country Fair in Oregon, hoping to give mushrooms another try, since I have been off of any medication for over 1 1/2 years. I ate 1/8th on a partially full stomache, and experienced the same exact effects as before - none. I was feeling a little queasy 3 hours after the dose, but I drank some water and felt better. After staying up for 5 hours waiting to see the light, I passed out from being too tired.
