I am experienced with using 25i-NBOMe and have tripped it around 15 times. I decided to test the limits and took a very high dose of 25i.

T +0:0 Dropped 2.5 25i blotters. Just listening to dubstep and practicing popping.

T +0:30 Feeling the come up of the NBOMe quite strong and there is no nausea. Music becoming more pleasurable.

T +1:00 At this point I am seeing strong visuals with flowing geometric patterns of yellow, red, blue, and green. Everything looks wavy.

T +1:30 Decided to take the trip to a new level. I busted out my Gods of Aroma 'Titan' spice (possibly JWH-018) out and packed two one hitters. I took the entire hit on both packs and held in for 15 seconds. Next thing I know my vision is completely taken over by the combination and I am left mindfucked very mildly worried about if I may have taken too much.

T 2:00 All uneasy feelings disapate and am staring in awe at the colorshow that is taking place on my wall. I decide to pack a bowl of weed and spice mixed together and proceeded to do so. I held the hits for 15 seconds a piece and got 3 good hits out of it. This is where things get hazy. Time no longer exists and I am attempting to make a sandwich. It took close to 30 minutes to make because I couldn't see anything clearly or think.

T 3:15 Drop another 1,000 microgram blotter and put in in upper lip not swallowing any saliva as before.

T 5:00 I am losing touch with reality as the next dose has kicked in. I am motionless, breathing mildly hard in my bed still watching the lightshow as I also pack another bowl of spice and weed and smoke it, doing the same as before.

T 5:15 I am COMPLETELY GONE. I can no longer see nor think logically. I stared out the window at the fence and at the top of the fence stood what looked like Geico Geckos dancing on top of it. I stared puzzled for quite some time.

T 6:30 Space Ragner has reported back to Earth. I am off the peak and pretty much down. No uneasy feelings.

T 7:15 Fall asleep.

Summary: 25i is one of my favorite trips and I wanted to see what it would do in higher doses. It was very overpowering. Overall a very clean trip with a very mild body load sometimes.
