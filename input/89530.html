Really Scary Combo
4-HO-MET, 2C-E & 6-APB
by Ravunker
Citation:   Ravunker. "Really Scary Combo: experience with 4-HO-MET, 2C-E & 6-APB (ID 89530)". Erowid.org. Feb 14, 2011. erowid.org/exp/89530

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 15 mg oral 4-HO-MET (powder / crystals)
  T+ 0:00 20 mg oral 2C-E (powder / crystals)
  T+ 0:50 115 mg oral 6-APB (powder / crystals)
  T+ 2:00 85 mg oral 6-APB (powder / crystals)
  T+ 4:00   oral Pharms - Eszopiclone  

BODY WEIGHT: 220 lb


A few days ago I had a really intense RC experience that I think changed my outlook on the whole scene now.

I recently found a great RC website that had quite a product line of exotic RC's. I made a large purchase, and received the goodies few days later.

I had taken 2C-E and 4-HO-MET before, but something went wrong this night.

I started off with taking 15mg of 4-HO-MET and 20mg of 2C-E, weighed out, and parachuted. After 50 minutes, I took 115mg of 6-apb, which was a a light tan color. After 1.5 hours of taking the first dose of anything, I started to feel the familiar waves of the phen/trypt combo. I was watching Family Guy and having a blast.

At the the 2 hour point, I didn't feel like I was rolling much with the initial dose of 6-apb, so I took another 85mg of it, parachuted, with some juice.

At the 2.5 hr mark, something felt very wrong. I looked over at my buddy, and told him 'Something isn't right'. At this point, the bad trip began for the rest of the night. Somewhere in the mix I did something wrong, and I started to feel really shitty. Increased heartbeat, bad paranoia and anxiety. The visuals I had were very sadistic, sinister type visuals, can't really explain it any other way. My right arm was going numb, and I had to constantly rub it and try not to lay down and give in. This horrible feeling lasted for 2 hours. It was a bad trip gone horribly wrong, and the idea of dying was constantly being circulated in my head.

After the crazyness, I procured some Lunesta to comedown, and started to gain control. After this point (4 hrs total later) I was in a cracky mood where I was all over the web, on multiple websites, tweakin out, listening to music, just trying to chill.

This night completely scarred me and I don't think I well ever do a combo night ever again without proper research.
