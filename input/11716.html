Where Do I Come From?
Salvia divinorum (5x extract)
by J.C
Citation:   J.C. "Where Do I Come From?: experience with Salvia divinorum (5x extract) (ID 11716)". Erowid.org. Jan 9, 2002. erowid.org/exp/11716

 
DOSE:   smoked Salvia divinorum (extract - 5x)

BODY WEIGHT: 175 lb


As always the words fail to convey the space but a rough image.

Having never experienced this specific Salvia before. I smoked twice after the first bowl had little effect. Soon perception altered in a most unfamiliar way. One moment on the couch in my workshop and the next in etherical space with many other being, uncountable thousands.Asort of circus or surfing a sea of energy.

Soon I become aware differently, with will, power. I stood in both realms knowing the room I was in and aware of the world that appeared to be above me and to the right. I chose to fully enter and experienced on a level of awareness that permiated my entire being. I was in part and the whole at once. After what felt to be 3 min. there was a fade I could see the room materializing beneath me and slightly to the left. Back in the room. little effect remained.
