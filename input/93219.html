Friend Was Feeling It, I Wasn't
Fluoxetmine & Mushrooms
by Soquel
Citation:   Soquel. "Friend Was Feeling It, I Wasn't: experience with Fluoxetmine & Mushrooms (ID 93219)". Erowid.org. Oct 14, 2011. erowid.org/exp/93219

 
DOSE: 40 mg oral Pharms - Fluoxetine (daily)
  4 g oral Mushrooms (dried)

BODY WEIGHT: 165 lb


I am prescribed 40mg of Prozac a day. I have been on Prozac for about 9 years, reaching 40mg/day almost a year ago.

At 5pm I ingested 2g of 'Puerto Rican Angels' mushrooms. My last trip on mushrooms had been over 2 years ago. At 5:45pm my friend was feeling it, I was not at all. I ate another gram. 6:10pm- still nothing. I took one last gram.

6:30pm I was tripping. A very nice trip. Slight distorted vision (faces in smoke and my room felt sloped). TONS of thought loops, epiphanies, and enlightenment. Overall a great trip. The one I have been looking for. Probably what someone not on an SSRI would get after only about 2.5 grams.
