Jackie the Jokeman
Poppies - Opium
by Stan Dup
Citation:   Stan Dup. "Jackie the Jokeman: experience with Poppies - Opium (ID 25793)". Erowid.org. Apr 10, 2006. erowid.org/exp/25793

 
DOSE: 15 oz oral Poppies - Opium (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 268 lb


So I tried poppy seed tea..

my wife was at work for 12 hours so I decided to try it. I went to my local supermarket and got 15 oz (equilviant to like 500g). I was shocked to realized (the average dose of 500g) is quite a bit!! like 2 WHOLE cups..I added about 15 oz of lemon juice (about half a liter) and let it sit for about 30 minutes while stirring every 5 minutes...

then I added 2 cups of boiling nestea, stirred and let sit for another 10 minutes. When I tried to strain it, coffee filters take too damn long, so I used an old shirt. I think I got about a little over 2 cups of fluid so I guickly guzzled it down...tastes BAD! like peanuts and lemon dispite the 7 packets of sweet and low I added.

I sat down and watched Jackie the Jokeman's video and waited for my buzz... in about 45 mins I started to get light headed and my shoulders numb and fuzzy...and just kinda tapered there.. I thought after 2-3 hours my buzz would increase but it never did. The buzz was equilivant to one percocet with 1 or 2 beers..not that strong. about 6 hours later I just got tired and went to sleep. I guess because of my weight this was too low of a dose, I kinda was dissapointed, all that for a buzz I could of gotten from 4-5 beers.
