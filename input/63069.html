Visine Worked
Cannabis & Drug Testing

Citation:   Lo. "Visine Worked: experience with Cannabis & Drug Testing (ID 63069)". Erowid.org. Sep 27, 2007. erowid.org/exp/63069

 
DOSE:   smoked Cannabis (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb


I just wanted to corroborate the use of Visine as a false negative producer when faced with a drug test. I smoked May 13 rather heavily then took my test 9am the next morning, adding approximately 5mL Visine to a 60mL urine sample. I was stunned when the test came back negative. I did not drink copious amounts of water of do anything different other than the adulterant. I also do not use any other drugs.
