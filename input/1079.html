Quiet Conversations
Wormwood
by Raventhorne
Citation:   Raventhorne. "Quiet Conversations: experience with Wormwood (ID 1079)". Erowid.org. Oct 13, 2000. erowid.org/exp/1079

 
DOSE:   oral Wormwood (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 210 lb


On a number of occasions I have brewed wormwood tea. Sometimes I add fresh catnip--generally I add fresh mint. 1/8 cup, more or less fresh chopped wormwood leaves. Often I'll add honey--wormwood is bitter.

It's interesting to share tea with a friend. The conversation gradually slows to a trickle. Occasional words punctuate the silence. Thoughts stop intruding. It's enough to simply sit together.

No groovy lights, no sense of lethargy. I could direct myself to action when required. But left to my own devices I get very quiet. The thoughts slow to a trickle. And then seem to stop. For long periods.

TV ruins the experience.
