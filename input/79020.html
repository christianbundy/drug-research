Fixes Stutter
MDMA (Ecstasy)

DOSE:   repeated   MDMA

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


I have taken MDMA a few times a year and my stutter completely goes away for the duration. I am not sure if it's because I am at total ease or because it actually does something neurologically, but all I can say is that I don't stutter at all. Makes me wish I could take a smaller dose regularly just to avoid stuttering. Though I doubt that is safe, so I won't try it, but I wish the medical profession took a look at this.
