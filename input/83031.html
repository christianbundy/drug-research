Speedball Testing
Heroin & Dimethocaine
by Zenix M.
Citation:   Zenix M.. "Speedball Testing: experience with Heroin & Dimethocaine (ID 83031)". Erowid.org. Sep 6, 2011. erowid.org/exp/83031

 
DOSE: 150 mg insufflated Dimethocaine (powder / crystals)
  150 mg IV Dimethocaine (liquid)
    IV Heroin (liquid)

BODY WEIGHT: 145 lb


A while back I ordered some dimethocaine. There was not much info on this substance. So I decided to contribute. Preliminary tests proved that intranasally, this substance did feel a lot like cocaine. Mild euphoria and moderate stimulation. It was better than bad cocaine, but not as good as amazing coke.

I decided to see it would replace the speedball. I put the last 150 mgs in solution and added one bag of 'Overdose' brand heroin.

:30 seconds warm feelings, sense of stimulation
:01 realized that there was not going to be a rush or any audio flanging.
:10 On irc, kind of disappointed
:30 Just feeling the heroin.

In conclusion, I feel that dimethocaine was interesting. But not altogether fulfilling. I'd do it again. But I wouldn't go out of my way to.
