Mild Euphoria
Morning Glory (Heavenly Blue)
by The Anarchist
Citation:   The Anarchist. "Mild Euphoria: experience with Morning Glory (Heavenly Blue) (ID 52192)". Erowid.org. Apr 10, 2007. erowid.org/exp/52192

 
DOSE: 6.0 g oral Morning Glory (ground / crushed)

BODY WEIGHT: 130 lb


I recently decided to try Morning Glories, hoping to achieve a psychedelic experience similar to those reported by LSD, mushroom, and mescaline users. I went to the local Wal-Mart, purchased four packs of Morning Glory seeds which contained 1.5 g each, went home, pounded the seeds with a hammer to break the shells, poured the crushed seeds into a glass of milk, and drank, at approximately 1:30 pm. I then returned to normal activities about the house, and waited for the seeds to take effect.

At around 3:00 pm, I began to feel tired and vaguely happy, for no discernable reason. These feeling remained throughout the day. I was slightly disappointed that I experienced no psychedelic effects, especially since the dose I took was deemed to be between medium and strong, and these psychedelic effects were supposed to be fairly pronounced. Nonetheless, I was very pleased with my experience, and will most likely pursue it again, as I was able to achieve mild euphoria without any noticeable impairment in my capabilities to perform normal tasks, and noticed no decrease in mental capabilities.

Here is a list of effects that I felt:
 Mild euphoria for a prolonged period of time (from about 3:00 pm to 12:00 am.). This was accompanied by increased appreciation of humor, and increased interest in matters that I normally find banal.
 Relaxation and tiredness.
 Slight increase in abnormal thinking.
 Slight increase in talkativeness.
 Mild nausea at the beginning (cured by one sip of Pepto Bismal), mild headache (cured by two Aspirin pills).
