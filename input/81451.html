I figured that lots of fillers from the pills passed through my filter and I slammed them too. This is now 20 hours later and feel horrible pain in my head and I am very weak, my legs shake and I feel moderate nausea. I also feel that I seriously lost some intelligence! This is horrible thing to experience and I wrote this to warn others about injection of pills with plenty of fillers.
