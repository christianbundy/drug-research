DOSE: 300 mg oral Pharms - Bupropion (daily)
  120 mg oral MDMA (powder / crystals)


I noticed an unexpected interaction between the wellbutrin I was taking and MDMA.

I had been taking 150mg wellbutrin 2x/day and when I took 120mg mdma, the effects took an extra 1/2 hour to kick in.

I tried it a few times and the results were the same, everyone else got off before i did! The intensity and type of effects from the MDMA were the same as they were when I wasn't on wellbutrin, not any stronger or weaker.
