Control your dreams
Calea zacatechichi
by Taylor
Citation:   Taylor. "Control your dreams: experience with Calea zacatechichi (ID 3127)". Erowid.org. Oct 20, 2000. erowid.org/exp/3127

 
DOSE: 1 cup oral Calea zacatechichi (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 155 lb


One day i decided to order some salvia from a website when i read a blurb about Calea zacatechichi. One ounce was only $15 so i went ahead and ordered it. I didn't try it for a few weeks after i got it because i was playing with the salvia (pretty fucked up stuff).

Anyway, one day i made some tea with the Calea, only using a fat pinch of leaves and sprouts. This stuff is so much more bitter than you could imagine, it's almost a trip to drink it! After a few minutes i felt a little relaxed, a very very subtle weed high feeling.

It was getting late so i decided to sleep, unaware of calea's dream enhancement. All i can say is damn! My dreams were so incredibly tripped out and realistic. They were very symbolic in a way, i remember every part of the long dream. At points i remember slightly waking up but still in dream-mode, and from there on i knew i was dreaming and could actually control my dreams. It was amazing, i would just start thinking about flying and there i was floating in the clouds.

The only bad part about calea was that the next day I felt really dazed. I went to school and i couldn't freaking concentrate on anything and it felt like anything anyone would say went in one ear and out the other. If i wasn't at school it wouldn't matter. Calea zacatechichi is very cool, the dreams are so incredibly meaningful it's not even funny. I'm going to go make some tea right now, i feel like dreaming up a storm tonight.
