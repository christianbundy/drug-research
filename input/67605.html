Gentle Euphoria
Melatonin
by Pseudo Nephren
Citation:   Pseudo Nephren. "Gentle Euphoria: experience with Melatonin (ID 67605)". Erowid.org. Jan 13, 2008. erowid.org/exp/67605

 
DOSE: 0.5 mg   Melatonin

BODY WEIGHT: 155 lb


On the night of this experience I ate some dark chocolate prior to dinner. Because I am still not sleepy at 10pm, I decided to take 0.5 mg of Melatonin to help me fall asleep. I think around 30 minutes I suddenly felt very fuzzy, and fell asleep soon after that.

I woke up around 3AM in the morning, and detected some unusual sensations immediately. I felt very relaxed, and comfortable. I could feel warm waves of gentle euphoria wash over me. I was cuddly as well, wanting to hug someone close to me. All this time I was sporting a semi-hard erection, but desire for sex was not very strong, just a desire for
a good hug.

These strong feelings subsided by around 5AM. I woke up a bit later than usual because of this episode, but was not too groggy. Needless to say, the whole experience was pleasant.
