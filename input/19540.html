Foxygasm
5-Meo-DiPT, Mushrooms & Cannabis
by Little Bunny Foo Foo
Citation:   Little Bunny Foo Foo. "Foxygasm: experience with 5-Meo-DiPT, Mushrooms & Cannabis (ID 19540)". Erowid.org. Jan 24, 2003. erowid.org/exp/19540

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 1 hit oral 5-MeO-DiPT
  T+ 0:08 1 hit oral 5-MeO-DiPT
  T+ 0:12 3.0 g oral Mushrooms

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


The night started around 9:00 pm when me, my roomate, and another friend dosed on foxy. We'd been smoking pot all day and continued smoking throughout the trips. I swallowed the pill and went to set up the video camera which taped most of our trip. About 9:30-9:45 I start to get really happy and can feel slight body effects so I sit down with a guitar and play a bunch of really random silly songs.

About 10:00 my trip started to kick in and I was feeling really euphoric. I leaned over to write a message on our dining table and by the time I got done writing, my world had gone insane. I completely lost track of time and visuals were everywhere. I felt almost a sort of tenseness in my chest that kind of pulsated all over my body. The only thing I could compare it to is the feeling you get achieving orgasm. Only, it didn't die off. It made me giggly and weird and I wanted to move around and hug things.

In my mind, I kept going over things that were happening around me. For example, I was obsessing over why I couldn't attend an all girl party next door. I came up with a million obsurd reasons like thinking they all hated me and that's why. Not giving thought to the fact that I was a male.
Come about 5 am, I took another strong dose, and had a trip slightly less intense and somewhat shorter, although generally the same. Before I could completely come down, I dosed on about 3 grams of shrooms and had a pretty intense shroom trip. The trip didn't give me the normal body high, but the visuals were crazy.

After being strung out for so long on hallucinagens, I eventually just smoked a bowl and passed out for 15 hours.
