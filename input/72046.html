Very Mild Sedative Effect
Albizia julibrissin (Mimosa Flower)
by Not a pot smoker
Citation:   Not a pot smoker. "Very Mild Sedative Effect: experience with Albizia julibrissin (Mimosa Flower) (ID 72046)". Erowid.org. Nov 27, 2009. erowid.org/exp/72046

 
DOSE: 7.5 g oral Albizia julibrissin (tea)

BODY WEIGHT: 200 lb


Made a warm tisane using 5-10g of Mimosa flower (not the bark, and *DEFINITELY NOT* the pods/legume of the tree). This seemed to have a *very* mildly sedative effect (tastes ok too), however, please note that this was a 1 time dose & not taken chronically as it's probably supposed to be taken as. I'm still experimenting with A.j. as well as a few other psychotropic herbs. Other herbs I've been tampering with (by using the whole herb & by making my own extracts) include Bacopa m., Crocus sativus, Scutellaria baicalensis, St. John's Wort and others.
