A Couple of Bad Glocks
Sold as Ecstasy
by Daniel Renegade
Citation:   Daniel Renegade. "A Couple of Bad Glocks: experience with Sold as Ecstasy (ID 100485)". Erowid.org. Jun 16, 2013. erowid.org/exp/100485

 
DOSE: 2 tablets oral Bad/Suspect Ecstasy (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 120 lb


I picked up two white ecstasy pills with a glock/pistol logo on them off my friend who tried them, and said they made her trip out sort of like 5-MeO-DiPT made her feel but stronger. She said the person who gave them to her said they were laced with LSD. I find this very hard to believe.

But being curious, and seeing as she did not like or want them, I purchased them from her for five bucks. Online reports have reported some of these to contain piperazines (although never tested) and one report suspected it contained a 2C- base with low MDxx content. Other reports said they were great. All I have learned is, pills are different everywhere, and hopefully it isnt piperazine. Nausea is no fun.

It's a Tuesday, Im home alone until Im guessing at least 6pm. I'm kind of nervous, but thats to be expected when you dont know exactly what youre taking.

2:24pm  Swallowed both with a cup of OJ

2:34  The usual butterflies in my stomach I get after taking pills is present

2:44  Mild nausea, feeling very slightly off baseline. Going to go take a hit from the bong to help my tummy

2:54  Go to the bathroom to throw up. Lasts about a minute.

2:58  Eyes feel sort of tight, if that makes sense. Starting to notice very slight visual distortions. Nausea is mostly gone.

3:04  feeling simultaneously hot and cold, mainly on my back. Probably just sunburnt. No change in visuals

3:25  Nausea stays at about a 3.5 (of 10), occasionally moving up to a 6.5, but I havent thrown up again. The visuals stay at the point where it feels like youre about to start a mushroom trip, but havent quite begun. Just minor blurring out of the corner of my sight and what not. Head feels slightly foggy.

3:28  Just had one of those full body pulses that rises up to the eyes, that usual happens to me on mushrooms or a fair amount of MDMA, although I feel almost no MDMA effects. Maybe this is starting to pick up.

*After this point, nothing new happened, just nausea and the occasional body pulse and slight visual distortions*

I felt fine the next day. My official guess on what the substance is, is going to be 5-MeO-DiPT. It made me feel just like it, and it tasted just as chemically and gross on purging it up.

I do not recommend taking a substance if you are not sure what is in it, a better idea would have been for me to buy a test kit, but I didnt have the time.
-DSR
