Best Lollipop Ever
Fentanyl & Cannabis
by First Timer
Citation:   First Timer. "Best Lollipop Ever: experience with Fentanyl & Cannabis (ID 52055)". Erowid.org. Mar 21, 2010. erowid.org/exp/52055

 
DOSE: T+ 0:00 800 ug oral Pharms - Fentanyl (cookie / food)
  T+ 2:30 1 bowl smoked Cannabis (plant material)

BODY WEIGHT: 140 lb


Tonight I enjoyed an 800 mcg fentanyl lollipop I recently acquired from a friend. I had no idea what to expect, because I have never experimented with Opiates - not even Vicodin. I've been a daily pot-smoker for 3 yrs. now and had not been smoking when I ate the lollipop. Heres a summary

I began feeling effects about 15 minutes after letting the berry-flavored lollipop sit between my gums and cheek. It was mostly a slightly warm feeling in my head it was kind of hard to see straight. I stood up and walked into my living room. I felt kind of wobbly and I was still working on the lollipop. It took me about an hour to finish the lollipop, and by the time I did I was feeling warm all over. I just sat and listened to reggae music. I thought to myself This drug is no big deal I just feel a little high, thats all. Soon I noticed certain noises around my house kept catching my attention more than usual. At one point I thought I heard a mouse crawling around in my kitchen (paranoia maybe?). I got up and walked around a little bit. I noticed I felt more euphoric, like I was really happy about something.

I sat down more and when I did, I could hardly keep my eyes open. I dont know why, I wasnt tired but for some reason I just could NOT keep my eyes open. That lasted maybe 15 mins and thats when I started feeling really excellent! Rushes of energy going through my veins for about 30-45 mins. It peaked with a 5-10 minute period of absolute bliss I had a happiness compared to when MDMA first hits me.

But then suddenly I could feel the incredible high coming to a slow. I have done E about 10 times before and Im used to the climax at the beginning of a 4-6 hr. trip. However with Fentanyl, I found it very odd that it was such a lengthy buildup and the climax of the high is closer the end of the trip. Total time of the Fentanyl experience was approx 2 ½ hrs.

The come down was yucky but short-lived. I felt kinda poisoned in the limbs and had about 5 minutes of nausea and shakiness. I smoked a bowl and that helped a lot with the come down. In fact, the weed brought the high back temporarily and eased the come down process. Now it has been 3 hrs. since I took it and I feel pretty normal other than being a little tired.
