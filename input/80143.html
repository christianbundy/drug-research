Double Edged Sword
Mirtazapine (Remeron)
by Paxvox
Citation:   Paxvox. "Double Edged Sword: experience with Mirtazapine (Remeron) (ID 80143)". Erowid.org. Jul 21, 2009. erowid.org/exp/80143

 
DOSE: 30 mg oral Pharms - Mirtazapine (daily)

BODY WEIGHT: 210 lb


Remeron (Mirtazapine) is a mixed blessing. The drug helps with sleep and anxiety issues, but is almost impossible to come off of w/o serious complications. I have tried several times to w/d from this medication without success. Every time I tried to lower the dose from 30 mgs, terrible side effects resulted. Anxiety, aggitation, tremor, insomnia, emotionality----you name it, resulted from reducing my daily dose. I wish that I had never gotten on this drug.
