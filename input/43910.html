My Body is 500 Years Old
Heimia salicolia (Sinicuichi)

Citation:   Lp. "My Body is 500 Years Old: experience with Heimia salicolia (Sinicuichi) (ID 43910)". Erowid.org. Dec 27, 2007. erowid.org/exp/43910

 
DOSE: 15 g oral Heimia salicifolia (tar / resin)

BODY WEIGHT: 250 lb


My god, I write this as I am experiencing one of the worst things ever. Sinicuichi, don't do it. I seems like 3 or 4 other people had the same report I'm going to give. I am SORE. My entire body aches. My fingers hurt as I type this. It is about 1.5 hours after ingestion. I have ingested about a spoonful of goop that was the result of boiling down 1 ounce of Sinicuichi leaves. There was a slight stoney feeling between the naseau and intense (And I MEAN INTENSE) muscle soreness. I hope my heart hangs on, as it's a muscle.

NOT WORTH IT.
