DOSE: T+ 0:00 1 shot oral Alcohol - Hard (liquid)
  T+ 0:30 1 bowl smoked Skullcap (plant material)
  T+ 0:35 5 mg oral Pharms - Zolpidem (pill / tablet)

BODY WEIGHT: 125 lb


Subject Specs

Healthy teen male
weight- 125 lbs
height- 5' 9'



Ambien/Skullcap

T-0:00- One bowl of skullcap smoked.

T+0:05- Took 5mg of ambien, slight effects from skullcap noticable.

T+0:10- Feeling quite wavy, but that is a normal effect from skullcap.

T + 0:23 - Not really much different effects, just sort of a wavyness, felling sort of tired.

T + 0:32 - Feeling very very spacey and very very tired, I got very frightened by a commercial for a scary movie. Also forgot to mention that I did a shot of straight gin about 30 minutes before the initial dosing. Considering going to sleep, school in the morning.

T + 0:40 - Feel very happy, not as tired. I'm putting a movie on and laying down. Typing is somewhat of a challange.

T + 1:00 -I seem to be coming down already, I'm still as tired as I was, but there is no more happyness or wavy feelings.

I think that Ambien could be a wonderful drug to experiment with, but I don't think my dose was quite high enough, or maybe the skullcap or alcholol interfeared with the effects.
