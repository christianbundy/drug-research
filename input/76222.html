Convulsions From Hell
Naltrexone, Lorazepam (Ativan) & Quetiapine (Seroquel)
by Tmannole
Citation:   Tmannole. "Convulsions From Hell: experience with Naltrexone, Lorazepam (Ativan) & Quetiapine (Seroquel) (ID 76222)". Erowid.org. Jul 21, 2010. erowid.org/exp/76222

 
DOSE: 10 mg   Naltrexone
      Pharms - Lorazepam
      Pharms - Quetiapine

BODY WEIGHT: 150 lb


OK...I've been an opiate addict for a number of years and I finally decided to get some help. After using suboxone to detox, I was put on 5mg of naltrexone. 10 minutes after taking it I go into a uncontrollable siezure-like spasm. Since I wasn't really certain of what might happen I end up goin to the ER at 1am.

While laying on the bed in ICU, I am bending backward so far that my feet are touching the back of my head. 5 min later, which seemed like 5 hours, the nurse runs in and gives me a shot of ativan which knocks me out cold. While asleep I was told that I was still convulsing, but I was happy I was asleep for a little of it.

The nurses couldn't realy do anything else about it so I went home around 2am. After arriving at my house, I took a serequil so I could sleep, which I soon did. All of this lasted about 4 hours which seemed like would never end. The next day I didn't get out of bed, didn't eat, and didn't talk to anyone. Since I worked out muscles I didn't even know I had my body felt like it was falling apart. It took me an entire week to become normal again.

Now I will never touch another opiate.
