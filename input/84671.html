Pretty Sure My Liver Almost Gave Up
Piperazines - Nefazodone
by Nyar
Citation:   Nyar. "Pretty Sure My Liver Almost Gave Up: experience with Piperazines - Nefazodone (ID 84671)". Erowid.org. Apr 2, 2010. erowid.org/exp/84671

 
DOSE: 600 mg oral Piperazines - Nefazodone

BODY WEIGHT: 160 lb


I had had a good experience with taking too much antidepressants before, and I was really depressed, so I decided to try it again.

Long story short, nothing happened other than I had a horrible headache and horrible stomach aches for about a day and a half. And then, for about a week afterward, my stomach would randomly just clench up for a couple minutes, and it hurt so bad that it doubled me over (and made it very difficult to drive).

Considering that this drug has been pulled out of the market in America due to it being linked to sudden liver failure, and also considering my relatively common abuse of too much alcohol, I'm lucky if my liver isn't screwed up for good. So, basically, don't do this.
